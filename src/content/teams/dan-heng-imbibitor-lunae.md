If you want to see Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae at his absolute peak, clearing elite enemy waves in a single rotation, this is the definitive composition.

**H2: Rotation Guide**

The rotation for this team is a precise sequence aimed at building Skill Points for Dan Heng while maximizing Energy and Action Advance.

**Standard Rotation (Example):**
1.  **Silver Wolf:** Uses her Skill on the priority elite enemy to implant Imaginary Weakness (if not already present) or to apply DEF shred.
2.  **Huohuo:** Uses her Skill on the ally with the lowest HP (often Dan Heng) to provide a heal over time and ATK buff.
3.  **Tingyun:** Uses her **Basic Attack** on an enemy. **This is critical.** It triggers her Talent, giving Dan Heng Action Advance.
4.  **丹恒·饮月:** Now acting, he uses his **Enhanced Basic Attack (3 SP)**. He will likely be at 2 or more stacks of his Talent, allowing him to blast multiple enemies.
5.  **Next Cycle:** Huohuo may use her Basic Attack to generate a Skill Point. Silver Wolf continues to debuff. Tingyun uses her Basic Attack *again* to Action Advance Dan Heng another time.
6.  **Burst Window:** When Tingyun has enough Energy, use her Ultimate **on Dan Heng**. This gives him Energy, a DMG buff, and another Action Advance. Combine this with Huohuo's Ultimate Energy restore. Dan Heng should now have enough Energy for his Ultimate.
7.  **丹恒·饮月:** Use his **Ultimate**. This deals massive AoE damage and applies Imprisonment, extending the vulnerability window on all enemies, especially those Silver Wolf has debuffed.

**Key Combos to Remember:**
*   **Tingyun Basic Attack -> Dan Heng Enhanced Basic:** The core Action Advance engine.
*   **Huohuo Ultimate + Tingyun Ultimate on Dan Heng:** Instant Energy top-up for a near-guaranteed Dan Heng Ultimate.
*   **Silver Wolf Implant -> Dan Heng Ultimate/Attack:** Ensures Break and Imprisonment on key targets.

**When to Use Ultimate:**
*   **Dan Heng:** Use when he is at full Energy and 2-3 Skill Points are available to immediately follow with an Enhanced Basic Attack. Best used after Imprisonment from a previous Ult to extend damage.
*   **Tingyun:** Use **only on Dan Heng** to maximize his uptime and damage.
*   **Huohuo:** Use during high team-wide damage phases or when the team needs an immediate Energy boost to unleash multiple Ultimates.
*   **Silver Wolf:** Use her Ultimate on the highest-HP elite enemy to shred its DEF before Dan Heng's burst window.

**H2: Strengths & Weaknesses**

**Best Matchups & Content:**
*   **Elite/Boss Fights:** Excels against bosses with Imaginary Weakness. Even without it, Silver Wolf makes this team viable.
*   **AoE-heavy Stages:** Dan Heng's blast attacks and AoE Ultimate thrive in waves of 3+ enemies.
*   **Memory of Chaos / Pure Fiction:** High burst damage and Imprisonment make it ideal for clearing stages within tight turn limits.

**Counters to Avoid:**
*   **Enemies that drain Skill Points:** Dan Heng consumes 3 SP per Enhanced Attack. Enemies or environmental effects that deplete your SP pool can cripple the team's rhythm.
*   **Heavy, unavoidable team-wide damage:** While Huohuo provides good healing, she is not a dedicated shielder. Fights with constant, high AoE damage can overwhelm her healing output.
*   **Content where Imaginary Break is ineffective:** Enemies immune to Imprisonment or with mechanics that negate Break effects reduce the team's crowd control advantage.

**Content This Team Struggles With:**
*   **Skill Point Starved Scenarios:** In fights where supports also need to use Skills frequently, the SP economy can become tight for Dan Heng's 3-SP attacks.
