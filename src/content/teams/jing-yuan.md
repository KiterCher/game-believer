# Best 景元 Team Guide | GameBeliever

## Team Overview
* **Team Type:** Hypercarry (with a DoT/Follow-up Twist)
* **Team Tier:** S-Tier (Premium) / A-Tier (Free-to-Play Alternative)
* **What Makes This Team Special:** This isn't just another Jing Yuan hypercarry team; it's designed to maximize the unique "Lightning Lord" mechanic through relentless turn manipulation and energy generation. While most Jing Yuan guides focus solely on buff stacking, this composition leverages specific supports to **accelerate his Ultimate**, ensuring his devastating lightning-lord field effect is active far more frequently than with standard teams. The core synergy revolves around Tingyun providing both ATK and Energy, and Bronya's skill **advancing Jing Yuan's action forward**, which in turn re-triggers the follow-up attack from his talent, "Lightning Lord." This creates a powerful feedback loop of damage, making this composition exceptional for clearing waves of enemies in content like **Memory of Chaos**.

## Team Members & Roles

**Core Team:**
1.  **景元 (Jing Yuan) - Main DPS:** The cornerstone of the team. His role is to accumulate stacks for his "Lightning Lord" via his Skill and Ultimate. His Ultimate is a massive AoE nuke that also instantly advances the Lightning Lord's action. **[Learn more about Jing Yuan's optimal build here: Jing Yuan Build Guide](/guides/jing-yuan-build)**
2.  **布洛妮娅 (Bronya) - Hypercarry Enabler:** The engine that makes this team tick. Her Skill, "Combat Redeployment," **advances Jing Yuan's turn by 100%** and grants a significant damage boost. This means Jing Yuan can use his Skill more frequently, rapidly building Lightning Lord stacks and generating energy. She is the best-in-slot support for this specific playstyle.
3.  **停云 (Tingyun) - Offensive Support & Battery:** Essential for two reasons. First, her Skill "Lotus' Delicacy" boosts Jing Yuan's ATK significantly. Second, her Ultimate **instantly restores 50 Energy** to Jing Yuan, dramatically reducing the time between his devastating Ultimates. Her follow-up attack also adds minor but welcome damage.
4.  **符玄 (Fu Xuan) - Sustain & Crit Buffer:** While any defensive support works, Fu Xuan is particularly synergistic. Her Matrix of Prescience provides a team-wide CRIT Rate increase, which benefits the often Crit-rate hungry Jing Yuan and his Lightning Lord immensely. Her health redistribution and minor healing ensure the team's survival without the need for a dedicated healer in most content. **A strong alternative is Huohuo for even more Energy and ATK buffs.**

**Key Alternatives:**
*   **For Bronya:** **艾丝妲 (Asta)** is a strong F2P alternative. Her Ultimate grants team-wide SPD, which helps Jing Yuan act faster. Her Skill also provides a universal ATK buff.
*   **For Fu Xuan:** **罗刹 (Luocha)** or **娜塔莎 (Natasha)** can be used for pure healing. **杰帕德 (Gepard)** offers strong shields.
*   **For Tingyun:** **花火 (Sparkle)** is a premium alternative who offers Skill Points and a massive damage boost through her Ultimate.

## Rotation Guide
This team's rotation is designed to generate Skill Points efficiently and funnel them into Jing Yuan and Bronya.

**General Combo Loop:**
1.  **Start-of-Battle:** Use Jing Yuan's Technique to apply a shock DoT to all enemies.
2.  **Turn 1 - Tingyun:** Use her Skill on Jing Yuan to grant him the ATK buff and Energy regeneration over time.
3.  **Turn 2 - Fu Xuan:** Activate her Ultimate or Matrix to establish survivability and CRIT Rate buffs.
4.  **Turn 3 - Bronya:** Use her **Skill on Jing Yuan**. This is the most crucial step. It will advance his turn immediately.
5.  **Turn 4 - Jing Yuan (Advanced Turn):** Now act immediately. **Use his Skill** to attack all enemies, dealing damage and building a stack for the Lightning Lord.
6.  **Next Cycle - Bronya:** Use her Basic Attack to generate a Skill Point and gain Energy for her Ultimate.
7.  **Next Cycle - Jing Yuan:** Use his Ultimate as soon as it's ready. This will deal massive AoE damage and immediately advance the Lightning Lord's action, triggering it for huge follow-up damage.
8.  **Repeat.**

**Key Combos & Ultimate Usage:**
*   **The "Overclock" Combo:** Tingyun uses Ultimate on Jing Yuan -> Bronya uses Skill on Jing Yuan -> Jing Yuan immediately uses Ultimate. This sequence can instantly fill the Lightning Lord's bar and trigger it for immense burst damage.
*   **When to use Ultimate:** Always use Jing Yuan's Ultimate when it is available, especially after Bronya has used her Skill on him. The goal is to have his Ultimate active as much as possible, as it both deals high damage and summons the Lightning Lord for a guaranteed, powerful attack.

## Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
*   **Exceptional AoE Damage:** Perfect for clearing groups of elite and trash mobs simultaneously.
*   **High Burst Potential:** With proper rotation, the combination of Jing Yuan's Ultimate and a fully stacked Lightning Lord can delete entire enemy waves.
*   **Skill Point Efficiency:** Jing Yuan and Bronya's core rotation is relatively SP-positive, allowing supports to use their skills comfortably.
*   **Versatile:** Excels in all forms of AoE-focused content, including **Pure Fiction, Memory of Chaos (with lightning-weak enemies), and standard event stages.**

**Weaknesses:**
*   **Vulnerable to CC:** If Jing Yuan or Bronya gets Crowd Controlled (stunned, frozen), the team's damage output plummets. **Fu Xuan's Matrix can help prevent this.**
*   **Reliant on Specific Supports:** The premium version of this team is heavily dependent on having both Bronya and Tingyun. Without them, the team's performance drops significantly.
*   **Single-Target Weakness:** While strong in AoE, the team lacks a dedicated single-target DPS option for bosses with no adds. In such cases, a Hunt or Destruction carry might be preferable.
*   **Enemy Toughness Bars:** Against enemies with Lightning resistance or high toughness, Jing Yuan's effectiveness is reduced. **Learn about other team types here: Team Composition Archetypes in HSR.**

## FAQ

**1. What is the best build for Jing Yuan in HSR?**
The best build for Jing Yuan focuses on boosting his CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK, and Lightning DMG. He benefits greatly from sets that increase follow-up attack damage and ultimate damage. For a detailed breakdown of his relic sets, substats, and recommended Light Cones, refer to our comprehensive **[Jing Yuan Build Guide](/guides/jing-yuan-build)**.

**2. What relics should I use for Jing Yuan?**
His best relic set is the **4-piece "The Ashblazing Grand Duke"** from the Prisoner in Deep Confinement cavern, as it directly boosts his follow-up attack and Ultimate damage. A strong alternative is a 2-piece mix of **"Salsotto" (Ult/Follow-up DMG)** and **"Band of Sizzling Thunder" (Lightning DMG)**. For Planar Ornaments, **"Inert Salsotto"** is his best-in-slot.

**3. What is Jing Yuan's best light cone?**
His signature Light Cone, **"Before Dawn,"** is unequivocally his best option. For 4-star alternatives, **"The Birth of the Self"** (from Herta's Store) is excellent as it boosts follow-up attack and Ultimate DMG. **"Sleep Like the Dead"** from the Battle Pass is also a strong choice. **See more options in our [Honkai Star Rail Light Cone Tier List](/tier-list/light-cones).**

**4. What team is best for Jing Yuan?**
The most popular and effective team is the hypercarry setup detailed in this guide: **Jing Yuan, Bronya, Tingyun, and a Sustain (Fu Xuan/Huohuo).** This team maximizes his turn frequency and Ultimate uptime. Other strong options include DoT-focused teams with Kafka or Break-focused F2P compositions.

**5. Is Jing Yuan good in Honkai Star Rail?**
Yes, Jing Yuan remains a very strong and versatile 5-star AoE DPS. His ability to deal both direct damage and sustained damage through his Lightning Lord makes him excellent for endgame content like Memory of Chaos and Pure Fiction, especially against lightning-weak enemies.

**6. What does Jing Yuan scale with in HSR?**
Jing Yuan's primary damage scaling comes from his **ATK stat**, followed by **CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG**. His Talent, Lightning Lord, also scales with his ATK and the number of stacks it has. His Ultimate has a high multiplier on his ATK. Investing in Lightning DMG is also highly effective.

**7. Is Jing Yuan a DPS or support?**
Jing Yuan is unequivocally a **Main DPS (Carry)**. His entire kit is designed to deal significant damage to enemies. While his Ultimate and Talent have unique mechanics, their purpose is offensive, not supportive.

**8. What is Jing Yuan's best F2P light cone?**
The best free-to-play Light Cone for Jing Yuan is **"The Birth of the Self,"** which can be purchased from Herta's Store in the Simulated Universe using Herta's Bonds. It provides a significant boost to both his Ultimate and follow-up attack damage, which are the core of his kit.

**9. How to build Jing Yuan for maximum damage?**
To build Jing Yuan for maximum damage: 1) Equip the **4-piece "The Ashblazing Grand Duke" relic set** and **"Inert Salsotto" ornament set**. 2) Prioritize **CRIT Rate > CRIT DMG > ATK% > Speed** on relic substats. 3) Use his signature Light Cone or "The Birth of the Self." 4) Pair him with supports like **Bronya and Tingyun** in a team composition designed to fuel his Ultimate and follow-up attacks. **Our [Jing Yuan Endgame Guide](/guides/jing-yuan-endgame) covers advanced optimization.**
