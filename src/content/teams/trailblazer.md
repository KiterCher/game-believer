## Team Overview

**Team type:** Break-Focused Hypercarry  
**Team tier:** S-tier (Specialized)  
**What makes this team special:** This isn't your standard hypercarry team. This composition leverages the Physical Trailblazer's innate capability to inflict **Bleed** and, more importantly, acts as a **dedicated Break enabler**. The team is built around maximizing Weakness Break damage, extending the Broken state, and piling on damage-over-time (DoT) effects while the enemy is vulnerable. It transforms the Trailblazer from a simple AoE physical dealer into the core of a "Break & Burn" strategy that excels in certain endgame content like Memory of Chaos (MoC) when facing Physical-weak enemies.

## Rotation Guide

The goal is to break enemies as fast as possible and then unleash hell during the vulnerability window.

1.  **Preparation Phase (Before Enemy Turn):**
    *   Use **Ruan Mei's Skill** ("String Slow") to activate her Break Efficiency and DMG buffs.
    *   Use **Silver Wolf's Skill** ("Allow Changes?") to implant **Physical Weakness** on the highest-priority target.
    *   Use **Gallagher's Basic Attack** to apply his healing-over-time and build energy.

2.  **Breaking Phase (Trailblazer's Turn & Ultimates):**
    *   Trailblazer uses **Basic Attack** or **Skill** on the primary target. With Ruan Mei's buff, this will chunk Toughness significantly.
    *   **Use Trailblazer's Ultimate** ("War-Flail Blast!") **after** Silver Wolf has implanted weakness and **before** the enemy's Toughness bar is broken. This ensures the Ultimate's AoE damage contributes to the Break and triggers Talent Bleed on multiple targets.
    *   As soon as an enemy's Toughness bar breaks, **activate Ruan Mei's Ultimate** ("Petals to Stream, Repose in Dream"). This freezes the Broken state timer and amplifies the Break DMG they take.

3.  **Capitalization Phase (During Broken State):**
    *   All characters focus attacks on the Broken target(s). Silver Wolf uses her Ultimate for massive DEF Shred and DMG. Gallagher uses his Enhanced Basic to deal more Toughness damage to adjacent enemies. Trailblazer continues attacking to exploit the vulnerability and refresh Bleed stacks.
    *   Use Gallagher's Ultimate to reapply **Besotted** and increase Break DMG taken right as an enemy's Weakness Break is about to expire or when a new target becomes priority.

**Key Combo:** Silver Wolf (Skill) -> Ruan Mei (Skill) -> Trailblazer (Ultimate + Skill) -> **BROKEN** -> Ruan Mei (Ultimate) -> All-out attack.

## Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
*   **Unmatched AoE Break Potential:** Can delete entire waves in MoC or pure Fiction when facing Physical-weak groups.
*   **Superior Damage Sustain:** The combination of Bleed DoT, Break DMG, and Gallagher's Besotted means damage continues even when it's not the Trailblazer's turn.
*   **High Control:** Extended Weakness Break duration from Ruan Mei acts as a form of crowd control.
*   **Excellent vs. Summoners:** AoE Break can cripple both the summoner and its minions simultaneously.

**Weaknesses:**
*   **Highly Specific:** Performs poorly against enemies without Physical Weakness unless Silver Wolf is present.
*   **Single-Target Bosses:** While still strong, the team's greatest advantage (AoE Breaking) is less impactful against a lone, high-HP boss.
*   **Bleed-Resistant Enemies:** Certain elite enemies or bosses (e.g., some Swarm or Automation enemies) resist Bleed, reducing one layer of damage.
*   **Reliant on Ultimates:** The strategy's peak power requires good energy management and Ultimate timing.

## Alternative Team Compositions

## FAQ

**1. What is the best build for Trailblazer in HSR?**  
For this Break team, focus on **Break Effect** and **Attack%** on Relics. The 4-piece *Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge* set is ideal, boosting Break Damage. Use an Attack% or Break Effect% Body, Speed or Attack% Boots, Physical DMG% or Attack% Sphere, and a Break Effect or Attack% Rope. Substats prioritize Break Effect, Speed, and Attack%.

**2. What relics should I use for Trailblazer?**  
As mentioned, the **4-piece *Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge*** is BiS for maximizing Break Damage. Alternatively, a **2-piece *Iron Cavalry* + 2-piece *Messenger Traversing Hackerspace*** can work for more Speed. For Planar Ornaments, **Talia: Kingdom of Banditry** (Break Effect) or **Inert Salsotto** (Ultimate & Follow-up DMG) are strong choices.

**3. What is Trailblazer's best light cone?**  
Their 5-star signature Light Cone from the Battle Pass, **Nowhere to Run**, is excellent for Break Effect and Follow-up DMG. For 4-star options, the free-to-play **Flows Like Midnight** (from the Herta Store) is fantastic, providing Break Effect and ATK%. **A Woof! Walk Straight!** (Gacha) is also very good for pure Break Effect.

**4. What team is best for Trailblazer?**  
As this guide details, a **Break-Focused Team** with Ruan Mei and Silver Wolf is currently their most powerful and specialized composition. However, they are versatile and can work in standard Physical Hypercarry or even DoT teams.

**5. Is Trailblazer good in Honkai Star Rail?**  
Yes, absolutely. While a free 4-star, they are a capable AoE Physical DPS who becomes **exceptional** in content tailored to their strengths, particularly when facing Physical-weak enemies. With the right team like this one, they can clear the hardest content in the game.

**6. What does Trailblazer scale with in HSR?**  
They primarily scale with **Attack%** and, in specialized builds like this one, **Break Effect%**. Their damage also scales with **Physical DMG Bonus**.

**7. Is Trailblazer a DPS or support?**  
Trailblazer is a **DPS** (Damage Per Second) character, specifically an **AoE Erudition-style** dealer from the Path of Destruction. Their kit is focused on dealing damage, not supporting allies.

**8. What is Trailblazer's best F2P light cone?**  
The **Flows Like Midnight** from the Herta Light Cone Superimposition Shop is the best free-to-play option, providing a massive Break Effect boost. **Swordplay** from gacha is also a strong generalist choice.

**9. How to build Trailblazer for maximum damage?**  
For this team, maximum damage comes from maximizing **Break Damage**. Equip the *Iron Cavalry* Relic set, prioritize Break Effect in substats, and use a Light Cone with Break Effect. Ensure they have enough **Speed** (at least 120-130 with buffs) to act frequently. In a traditional hypercarry build, stack Attack% and Crit Rate/Damage instead.

**10. Is Trailblazer worth pulling in 2026?**  
Trailblazer is a free character you obtain early in the story, so there's no need to pull for them. The question might be if they're **worth building and investing in**, and the answer is yes, especially if you enjoy Break-style teams or need a strong free AoE Physical DPS.
