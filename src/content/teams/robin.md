# Best Dr. Ratio Follow-Up Team Guide with Robin | GameBeliever

## Team Overview

**Team type:** FollowUp Hypercarry (with Dr. Ratio as the main DPS)  
**Team tier:** S-Tier  
**What makes this team special:** This composition transforms Dr. Ratio from a strong single-target DPS into a relentless machine gun of follow-up attacks. The core synergy revolves around Robin's unique ability to grant energy to the entire team after her Ultimate and amplify follow-up attack damage. Unlike generic support setups, Robin here isn't just a buffer; she is the engine that fuels Dr. Ratio's and Aventurine's counterattacks, creating a cascade of damage that can overwhelm even the toughest single-target bosses.

## Team Members & Roles

This follow-up attack team is built around triggering Dr. Ratio's Talent and Ultimate as frequently as possible. Here’s the core lineup and the roles each member plays:

**1. Dr. Ratio (Main DPS)**
*   **Role:** Primary follow-up attacker.
*   **Why he’s here:** Dr. Ratio’s kit is designed to unleash follow-up attacks when debuffs are applied to enemies. The entire team structure is built to provide consistent debuffs (from himself, Aventurine, and Topaz) and to maximize his energy generation, allowing him to spam his Ultimate.
*   **Key Contribution:** Massive single-target and blast follow-up attack damage.

**2. Robin (Harmony Support - Core Enabler)**
*   **Role:** Ultimate Battery, Team-wide Buffer, Follow-up Attack Amplifier.
*   **Why she’s here:** Robin is the lynchpin. Her Ultimate, "Vow of the Deep," not only deals damage but also advances all allies' actions by 25% and grants them Energy. This is crucial for getting Dr. Ratio’s Ultimate faster. Furthermore, her Talent significantly increases the damage of follow-up attacks, making her the perfect synergistic support for this specific team archetype. She converts ATK into a party-wide damage bonus, benefiting all attackers.
*   **What she contributes:** Team-wide ATK buff, massive follow-up attack DMG boost, energy battery, and action advance.
*   **Alternatives:** **Ruan Mei** (for Break Efficiency and general DMG buffs) or **Tingyun** (for direct ATK and Energy funneling to Dr. Ratio), though neither replicates Robin’s specific follow-up attack synergy.

**3. Aventurine (Sustain/Sub-DPS)**
*   **Role:** Shielder, Follow-up attacker, Debuffer.
*   **Why he’s here:** Aventurine is more than a defensive unit. His Talent allows him to launch a follow-up attack against enemies that hit his shielded allies, which counts as a debuff application for Dr. Ratio. His Ultimate also inflicts a debuff that increases follow-up attack damage taken. This dual-function role of protecting the team while contributing to the offensive engine is why he's ideal here.
*   **What he contributes:** Strong shields, consistent follow-up attack damage, crucial debuffs for Dr. Ratio's Talent.
*   **Alternatives:** **Gepard** or **Fu Xuan** (for pure preservation) but you lose the off-turn follow-up attacks and debuffs. **Lynx** or **Huohuo** (if you need a healer), but Huohuo's energy support is more generalist.

**4. Topaz and Numby (Debuffer/Sub-DPS)**
*   **Role:** Follow-up attack amplifier, Debuffer,副C.
*   **Why she’s here:** Topaz's "Proof of Debt" debuff increases the follow-up attack damage taken by the marked enemy, directly buffing both Dr. Ratio and Aventurine. Her own follow-up attacks from Numby generate significant energy for the team and provide additional damage.
*   **What she contributes:** Dedicated follow-up attack vulnerability debuff, high personal damage from follow-ups, additional energy generation.
*   **Alternatives:** **Black Swan** (for general DMG vulnerability and DoT) or **Silver Wolf** (for single-target DEF shred), though they don't specifically buff follow-up attacks.

## Rotation Guide

The goal is to chain ultimates and follow-up attacks in a specific sequence to maximize Dr. Ratio's damage output.

1.  **Setup Phase (First 1-2 Cycles):** Use Skill Points on Aventurine to ensure shields are up. Topaz uses her Skill to apply "Proof of Debt" on the priority target. Dr. Ratio uses his Skill. Robin uses her Skill to buff the team's ATK.
2.  **Core Combo - The "Symphony of Follow-Ups":**
    *   **Step 1:** Use **Robin's Ultimate**. This advances all allies, grants energy, and activates the follow-up attack DMG boost.
    *   **Step 2:** Immediately after Robin's action, use **Dr. Ratio's Ultimate** on the "Proof of Debt" target. This triggers his follow-up attack.
    *   **Step 3:** During the same cycle, **Aventurine** should have his Ultimate ready. Use it on the same target to apply his debuff, triggering another follow-up from Dr. Ratio.
    *   **Step 4:** If **Numby** acts next, his advance forward mechanic can trigger even more follow-up attacks.
3.  **Key Combo to Remember:** The sequence is **Robin Ult → Dr. Ratio Ult → Aventurine Ult**. This ensures all follow-up amplifications are active and debuffs are stacked for maximum effect.
4.  **When to use Ultimate:**
    *   **Robin:** Use as soon as it's ready, preferably at the start of a damage cycle.
    *   **Dr. Ratio:** Hold until after Robin's Ultimate to benefit from the energy and action advance. Always target an enemy with debuffs.
    *   **Aventurine:** Use either to pre-emptively apply the debuff or as a follow-up to Dr. Ratio's Ultimate.

## Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
*   **Exceptional Single-Target Damage:** This team excels inboss fights and elite enemy encounters where focus fire is key.
*   **High Action Economy:** Robin's Ultimate + Aventurine's and Numby's follow-ups mean you get many actions per cycle.
*   **Synergistic Burst Windows:** The team's damage peaks are incredibly high when ultimates are aligned.
*   **Low Skill Point Consumption:** Dr. Ratio, Aventurine, and Robin can all operate comfortably on a low SP budget.

**Weaknesses:**
*   **Vulnerable to Disruption:** If enemies can dispel debuffs (like the "Proof of Debt"), Dr. Ratio's follow-up rate drops significantly.
*   **Struggles with AoE Content:** While Dr. Ratio can hit adjacent targets, this team is primarily single-target focused. It's less effective against swarms of enemies compared to teams like DoT or Ruan Mei/Blade.
*   **High Investment Core:** The core trio (Dr. Ratio, Robin, Topaz) consists of 5-star characters, which can be difficult to acquire.
*   **Reliant on Enemy Turns:** Aventurine's follow-ups require enemies to attack shielded allies, which can be inconsistent against enemies with low speed or AoE that breaks shields.

**Best Matchups:** Single-target bosses (e.g., Phantylia, Cocolia in Memory of Chaos), Elite enemies with mechanics that require breaking or focused damage.
**Content to Avoid:** Pure Fiction (AoE-focused endgame mode), or stages with numerous enemies that apply frequent, strong AoE damage.

## Alternative Team Compositions

While the Dr. Ratio follow-up team is a top-tier option, Robin's flexibility allows her to slot into other powerful compositions.

1.  **The "Premium" DoT Team:**
    *   **Team:** Kafka, Black Swan, Robin, Huohuo.
    *   **When to use:** Against enemies weak to Lightning and Wind. Robin's team-wide buffs and action advance benefit both Kafka and Black Swan, accelerating DoT application. Use this for a more AoE-oriented approach.

2.  **The Break Team:**
    *   **Team:** Firefly, Ruan Mei, Harmony Trailblazer, Gallagher.
    *   **When to use:** When facing enemies weak to Fire/Imaginary or in the Apocalyptic Shadow endgame mode. Robin is **not** optimal here; Ruan Mei is the cornerstone for Break Efficiency. This is an example of a team where Robin is a sidegrade at best.

3.  **Budget "F2P" Follow-Up Variant:**
    *   **Team:** Dr. Ratio, Robin, Asta, Gepard/Lynx.
    *   **When to use:** If you lack Topaz or Aventurine. Asta provides Speed and ATK buffs, and her fire damage can sometimes apply a debuff. Gepard/Lynx provides pure sustain. The follow-up synergy is weaker, but the core of Robin boosting Dr. Ratio remains.

## FAQ

**1. What is the best build for Robin in this follow-up team?**
For this specific team, prioritize ATK% on the body, ATK% or SPD on boots, ATK% on the sphere, and Energy Regeneration Rate on the rope. Her buff scales off ATK, and you want her Ultimate up as often as possible.

**2. What relics should I use for Robin in this team?**
The **4-piece Musketeer of Wild Wheat** set is excellent for the ATK% and SPD bonus. Alternatively, the **2-piece/2-piece combo of Passerby of Wandering Cloud (ATK%) and Thief of Shooting Meteor (ATK%)** can work. For Planar Ornaments, **Penacony, Land of the Dreams** is the best in slot for its Energy and damage buff for Follow-up attacks.

**3. What is Robin's best light cone for this team?**
Her signature light cone, **"Flowing Nightglow,"** is the undisputed best, providing ATK, Energy, and a powerful team DMG buff. The best alternative is the 4-star **"Memories of the Past"** for the Energy Regeneration Rate, ensuring faster Ultimates.

**4. What team is best for Robin in Honkai Star Rail?**
Robin's best teams are those that feature multiple characters who deal significant follow-up attack damage, such as the **Dr. Ratio, Topaz, and Aventurine team** outlined here. She also shines in teams that can take advantage of her action advance, like dual-DPS setups.

**5. Is Robin a DPS or support?**
Robin is a **Harmony support**, but she is a unique "sub-DPS" hybrid. She provides powerful buffs but also contributes personal damage, especially to follow-up attacks. Her scaling makes her build focused on ATK, similar to a DPS.

**6. What does Robin scale with in HSR?**
Robin's main stat for her buffs is **ATK**. The damage of her Ultimate and the potency of her ATK buff for the team both scale with her ATK stat. Energy Regeneration Rate is also crucial to maintain Ultimate uptime.

**7. Is Robin worth pulling in 2026 or later?**
If you have Dr. Ratio, Topaz, or plan to play any follow-up attack-centric team, Robin's value remains very high due to her unique amplification niche. For general support needs, other Harmony units like Ruan Mei might be more versatile.

**8. How to build Robin for maximum damage in this team?**
To maximize Robin's personal and supportive damage, stack **ATK%** on every possible relic slot, use her signature light cone, and ensure she has an Energy Regeneration Rate rope. The **Penacony Planar set** is essential for boosting follow-up attack damage.
