# 景元 Build Guide — Best Relics, Light Cones & Teams | GameBeliever

## 景元 Build Overview

**Character:** 景元 (Jing Yuan)  
**Rarity:** 5★  
**Element:** Lightning  
**Path:** Erudition  
**Role:** AoE DPS, follow-up attack specialist.

Jing Yuan is one of the most powerful and versatile AoE damage dealers in Honkai: Star Rail, defining a unique playstyle centered around his powerful summon, the **Lightning-Lord**. Unlike traditional Erudition characters who spam their skills, Jing Yuan’s build philosophy revolves around managing his Skill stacks to empower the Lightning-Lord’s devastating follow-up attacks. This makes his output more burst-oriented and strategically timed.

**Why this build works:** This guide focuses on maximizing the damage of the Lightning-Lord, which accounts for a significant portion of Jing Yuan’s total damage output. By prioritizing stats and sets that amplify both his personal damage and the summon’s strikes, we create a synergistic engine. Jing Yuan himself possesses strong multi-target abilities on his Skill and Ultimate, but the true powerhouse is the Lightning-Lord, which attacks after Jing Yuan uses his Ultimate or gains stacks from his Talent. Therefore, an ideal 景元 build will stack ATK%, CRIT stats, and Lightning DMG to ensure every strike from him and his summon hits as hard as possible.

**Stat Prioritization:**
1.  **CRIT Rate & CRIT DMG:** Essential for any DPS. Aim for a balanced ratio.
2.  **ATK%:** Directly increases both Jing Yuan’s and the Lightning-Lord’s damage.
3.  **Lightning DMG Bonus:** For his personal attacks and some of the Lightning-Lord’s hits.
4.  **Speed (SPD):** Important for turn order and accumulating stacks. A specific breakpoint of 120+ is often recommended to ensure he can act enough times before the Lightning-Lord’s turn to build 6+ stacks.

## Best Relics for 景元

### Best 4pc Set

**Band of Sizzling Thunder (4pc)**
*   **Why:** This is the definitive best-in-slot set for Jing Yuan. The 4-piece bonus provides a substantial ATK% boost after using a Skill, which Jing Yuan does frequently to build stacks for the Lightning-Lord. This buff directly translates to higher damage for both him and his summon.
*   **Set Bonus Explanation:**
    *   2pc: Increases Lightning DMG by 10%.
    *   4pc: After using a Skill, the wearer’s ATK is increased by 25% for 2 turns. This perfectly synergizes with his core gameplay loop.

### Alternative Sets

**2pc + 2pc Combinations:**
If you lack a good 4-piece Band of Sizzling Thunder, mix-and-match sets can be very effective.
*   **Band of Sizzling Thunder (2pc) + Musketeer of Wild Wheat (2pc):** The best 2+2 combo. Provides a solid baseline of 10% Lightning DMG and 12% ATK. Use this until you farm a superior 4-piece set.
*   **Band of Sizzling Thunder (2pc) + Eagle of Twilight Line (2pc):** Use this if your substats are severely lacking Speed. The 6% SPD bonus can help you hit important breakpoints.

**When to use each:** The 4pc Band set is the long-term goal. The 2pc+2pc mixes are transitional or for players who haven't fully farmed the dedicated set.

### Best Planar Ornaments

**Inert Salsotto (2pc) - Best-in-Slot**
*   **Why:** Jing Yuan and the Lightning-Lord both deal significant follow-up attack damage, and his Ultimate is a major part of his burst. The 8% CRIT Rate and 15% increase to Ultimate and follow-up attack damage is tailor-made for him. This is the superior choice.

**Rutilant Arena (2pc)**
*   **Alternative:** A strong generic DPS option. If you can maintain 70%+ CRIT Rate, this provides a 20% Basic ATK and Skill DMG boost. However, since a larger portion of Jing Yuan's damage comes from the Lightning-Lord's follow-up attacks (which are not affected by this set), Inert Salsotto remains the top recommendation.

**Space Sealing Station (2pc)**
*   **Alternative:** A solid, unconditional option that provides ATK% at 120 SPD. It’s easier to activate and works well if you’re struggling to hit CRIT Rate goals for Inert Salsotto.

### Recommended Main Stats

*   **Body:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (Choose whichever balances your ratio best, aiming for ~1:2).
*   **Feet:** **Speed (SPD)** is highly recommended to ensure Jing Yuan can use his Skill often enough to stack the Lightning-Lord to 6+ stacks. ATK% is viable only with exceptional Speed substats or Speed-focused supports like Asta or Hanya.
*   **Planar Sphere:** Lightning DMG Bonus.
*   **Link Rope:** ATK% is the standard choice. An Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) Rope can be considered for more frequent Ultimates to summon the Lightning-Lord faster, but this is generally less optimal than raw ATK%.

### Substats Priority

1.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG**
2.  **ATK%**
3.  **Speed (SPD)** - Until breakpoint is met.
4.  **Break Effect** - A decent bonus for triggering Shock.

**Minimum Breakpoints:** There are no hard "minimum" substats, but aiming for a well-balanced character at Level 80 means targeting at least **1,200-1,300 ATK**, **70%+ CRIT Rate**, and **140%+ CRIT DMG** with Speed at **120+** before team buffs.

## Stats Breakpoints & Targets

*   **CRIT Rate Target:** **70-80%**. This is the sweet spot to activate the bonus from the Rutilant Arena set if you use it, and ensures consistent critical hits.
*   **CRIT DMG Target:** **140-160%+**. Aim for roughly double your CRIT Rate. With good relic substats and a 5★ Light Cone, this is achievable.
*   **SPD Breakpoints:** **120** is the primary target. This ensures Jing Yuan gets enough turns to build 6+ stacks for the Lightning-Lord before its turn arrives, maximizing its damage. **134** is a secondary breakpoint for an additional turn in the first two cycles of Memory of Chaos.
*   **ATK Target:** With Level 80 character, Light Cone, and ATK% boots/rope, aim for **2,500-2,800+** ATK before team buffs.
*   **Energy Regeneration:** With an ERR Rope, Jing Yuan needs 4 uses of his Skill or 2 uses of his Ultimate to recharge. Without it, it takes roughly 5 Skills or 2.5 Ultimates.

## Best Team Compositions

Jing Yuan thrives with supports who can buff his ATK, provide Speed boosts (to build stacks), generate Skill Points, and debuff enemies.

### Premium Team
**Jing Yuan + Tingyun + Bronya + Luocha (or Fu Xuan)**
*   **Why it works:** This is the gold standard.
    *   **Tingyun:** Provides massive ATK buffs and Energy, allowing for more frequent Ultimates and a stronger Lightning-Lord. Her Skill buff also lasts until the buffed character's next turn, covering the Lightning-Lord's attack.
    *   **Bronya:** The ultimate enabler. Her Skill advances Jing Yuan's action, letting him use his Skill more often to build stacks and instantly trigger the Lightning-Lord's turn. Her ATK% and CRIT DMG buffs are also phenomenal.
    *   **Luocha/Fu Xuan:** A sustain who uses minimal Skill Points, ensuring Jing Yuan and his supports have enough to use their abilities every turn.

### F2P Team
**Jing Yuan + Asta + March 7th/Natasha + Sushang/Support**
*   **Why it works:**
    *   **Asta:** Her Ultimate provides a huge team-wide Speed boost, helping Jing Yuan reach high SPD without Speed boots. Her Skill also gives a consistent ATK% buff.
    *   **March 7th / Natasha:** Basic sustain options. March can provide shields and occasional freezes, while Natasha offers cleansing and healing.
    *   **Flex Slot:** Can be another sub-DPS like Sushang or a debuffer like Pela.

### Alternative Teams
*   **Jing Yuan + Silver Wolf + Flex + Sustain:** Inserting Silver Wolf allows Jing Yuan to be effective even against non-Lightning-weak enemies by implanting the weakness.
*   **Jing Yuan + Hanya + Flex + Sustain:** Hanya is a powerful 4-star support who provides SPD, ATK%, and DMG buffs, acting as a more accessible alternative to Bronya/Tingyun.

## Gameplay Tips & Rotation

**Core Mechanic:** The **Lightning-Lord** has a base SPD of 60 and an initial stack count of 3. It gains stacks from Jing Yuan's:
1.  **Basic Attack:** +1 Stack
2.  **Skill:** +2 Stacks (max stack count is 10)
3.  **Ultimate:** Immediately causes the Lightning-Lord to act, using all current stacks.

**Skill Rotation:**
1.  **Opening:** Use Technique before battle to start with 1 free Skill point and Shock on enemies.
2.  **Ideal Goal:** Build to **6+ stacks** before the Lightning-Lord's turn for maximum damage (6 hits). With Tingyun or Bronya, reaching 8-10 stacks is possible for devastating damage.
3.  **General Flow:**
    *   Use Skill on Jing Yuan's turn to gain +2 stacks.
    *   Use Ultimate **when the Lightning-Lord's turn is next or very soon** to immediately trigger its attack with current stacks. This prevents the Lightning-Lord from "wasting" a turn if you summon it early.
    *   If your SPD is low or you have no Speed buffs, you may need to use Basic Attacks to conserve Skill Points and gain +1 stack, but this is a DPS loss. Prioritize SPD to allow Skill usage every turn.

**Key Combos:**
*   **Tingyun Combo:** Apply Tingyun's Skill buff -> Jing Yuan uses Skill -> Tingyun uses Ultimate on Jing Yuan -> Jing Yuan uses Ultimate to summon Lightning-Lord with high stacks and a massive ATK buff.
*   **Bronya Combo:** Bronya uses Skill on Jing Yuan -> Jing Yuan advances and uses Skill -> This quickly builds stacks and can manipulate the Lightning-Lord's turn order.

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
1.  **Using Ultimate Too Early:** Don't use Jing Yuan's Ultimate as soon as it's ready. Wait for the Lightning-Lord to be close to acting to maximize stack damage.
2.  **Ignoring SPD:** Building no SPD and using ATK boots may seem tempting for higher personal damage, but it severely handicaps your ability to build stacks, resulting in lower overall team damage.
3.  **Poor Skill Point Management:** Ensure your supports are SP-positive (using Basic Attacks often) so Jing Yuan can use his Skill every single turn.

## Investment Priority

*   **E0 Viable?** **Absolutely.** Jing Yuan is fully functional and powerful at Eidolon 0. His kit is complete.
*   **Key Eidolons:**
    *   **E1:** A decent boost, increasing the DMG multiplier of his Basic ATK, Skill, and Ultimate. Good but not required.
    *   **E2:** Increases the number of hits on the Lightning-Lord by 1. This is a significant damage increase but again, not necessary.
    *   **E6:** Massively increases the multiplier of the Lightning-Lord's attack, making it truly terrifying. A huge luxury upgrade.
*   **Light Cone Priority:** **Before Dawn > Night on the Milky Way (Herta Shop)**. Getting his signature Light Cone from the gacha is a major power spike. The free 5★ from Herta's Store is the best F2P investment. If you have neither, the 4★ "The Battle Isn't Over" or "Eyes of the Prey" are excellent stopgaps.
