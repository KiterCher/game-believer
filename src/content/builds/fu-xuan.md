# H1: 符玄 Build Guide — Best Relics, Light Cones & Teams | GameBeliever

## H2: Fu Xuan Build Overview
**Role:** 5★ Quantum Preservation Tank  
**Path:** Preservation  
**Element:** Quantum  
**Core Function:** Team damage mitigation, health redistribution, and providing a CRIT DMG buff.

Fu Xuan is not your traditional shielder like [March 7th](link-to-march-guide). Instead, she excels as a **mitigation tank**. Her unique Skill, Matrix of Prescience, redirects a percentage of all damage taken by her allies to herself, while also boosting their max HP and providing a significant CRIT DMG buff. Her Talent, Can One Foretell, further bolsters her survivability by granting her a stack of Formidable Fixation each time she or an ally takes damage, healing her when her HP falls below a threshold.

**Why this build works:** This guide prioritizes maximizing Fu Xuan's survivability and uptime to ensure her Matrix is always active. We focus on **HP%** to increase her personal durability and the max HP she provides, **SPD** to allow more turns for skill point generation and energy funneling, and **Energy Regeneration Rate** to keep her 100-cost Ultimate ready for emergency heals and Quantum toughness damage. The [Longevous Disciple] relic set is perfect as it converts her high HP pool into more DEF, creating a synergistic loop.

**What stats to prioritize:**
1.  **HP%:** Primary source of her tanking power and team buff.
2.  **SPD:** More actions = more skill points, more energy, more talent stacks.
3.  **DEF% / Effect RES:** Secondary survivability stats.
4.  **Energy Regeneration Rate:** For consistent Ultimate uptime.

## H2: Best Relics for Fu Xuan

### H3: Best 4pc Set
**Set:** **Longevous Disciple (4pc)**
- **Why:** This is the definitive BiS set. The 2pc bonus grants a flat HP increase, directly synergizing with her HP-scaling kit. The 4pc bonus is transformative: when Fu Xuan is hit, she gains a stack of Grit that increases her DEF by 8% for 2 turns, stacking up to 4 times. This converts her already massive HP pool into exceptional physical survivability, making her nearly unkillable. The condition is easily met due to her Matrix redirecting damage.

### H3: Alternative Sets
**2pc + 2pc Combinations:**
- **Guard of Wuthering Snow (2pc) + Longevous Disciple (2pc):** Provides a balanced boost of 12% max HP and 8% DEF. A strong, flexible option if you lack a good 4pc Longevous set.
- **Guard of Wuthering Snow (2pc) + Passerby of Wandering Cloud (2pc):** The classic tank setup. Grants 12% max HP and a valuable 10% Energy Regeneration Rate. Use this if your substats are poor and you need to guarantee Ultimate uptime.
- **When to use:** Use 2pc combos as transitional gear while farming for the 4pc Longevous Disciple set. The 4pc is always preferred for endgame content.

### H3: Best Planar Ornaments
- **Best-in-SSlot (BiS): Fleet of the Ageless.** The 8% max HP is perfect. The 2-piece set effect provides a passive 8% ATK buff to all allies when Fu Xuan's SPD is ≥120, a threshold easily achievable. This adds offensive utility to her defensive kit.
- **Alternative 1: Broken Keel.** If you can reach ≥50% Effect RES (achievable with subs), this grants 10% CRIT DMG to all allies. A potent offensive boost, especially for hypercarry teams.
- **Alternative 2: Penacony, Land of the Dreams.** Good for mixed Quantum teams, granting 10% Quantum DMG to the wearer and 5% to all Quantum allies. Primarily used in dedicated Quantum DPS teams.

### H3: Recommended Main Stats
- **Body:** **HP%.** Non-negotiable. Fu Xuan does not need CRIT stats; her Ultimate's damage is negligible compared to her tanking role.
- **Feet:** **SPD.** Essential for action frequency. **HP%** is an acceptable alternative if you already reach critical SPD breakpoints (see below) via substats alone.
- **Sphere:** **HP%.** The primary choice. **Quantum DMG%** can be considered for a slight personal damage boost, but HP% is vastly superior for team survivability.
- **Rope:** **Energy Regeneration Rate.** Highly recommended to maintain Matrix uptime. **HP%** is a viable fallback if you have exceptional SPD substats and an ERR rope is unavailable.

### H3: Substats Priority
**Priority Order:**
1.  **SPD:** Aim for breakpoints (see Section 4).
2.  **HP%:** Every roll increases her tanking and team buff.
3.  **Effect RES:** To resist debuffs that could disable her (e.g., imprisonment, freeze).
4.  **DEF%:** A minor but welcome survivability stat.
5.  Flat HP: Better than useless stats like ATK%.

**Minimum Breakpoints:** Aim for at least 2-3 rolls into SPD and HP% on each relic piece to be effective in endgame content like [Memory of Chaos](link-to-moc-guide).

## H2: Best Light Cones for Fu Xuan

### H3: 5★ Light Cones
- **Signature: She Already Shut Her Eyes.** The undisputed best. Provides a massive 24% max HP boost at S1, increases her DEF when her HP is low, and most importantly, grants her the "Knowledge" state which heals all allies for 80% of her HP when she uses her Ultimate. This adds a powerful team-wide heal to her kit, solving her main weakness.
- **Other 5★ Options:**
    - **Moment of Victory (Gepard's Sig):** Excellent defensive stats (DEF%) and aggro increase. The DEF% conversion to ATK is wasted, but the survivability is top-notch.
    - **Texture of Memory (Herta Shop 5★):** A great F2P-adjacent option. Provides a shield based on her DEF when she falls below 50% HP, adding another layer of mitigation.

### H3: 4★ Light Cones
- **Best 4★ Choices:**
    - **We Are Wildfire:** The best 4★ option. Increases the wearer's max HP and reduces damage taken by all allies at the start of battle. The HP increase is perfect.

- **Event Light Cones:** **Past and Future (from "Starhero Hunt" event)** is a good budget support cone, but it provides CRIT DMG, which isn't Fu Xuan's focus. We Are Wildfire is generally better.

## H2: Stats Breakpoints & Targets
- **CRIT Rate/DMG:** **Not a priority.** Fu Xuan is a tank; her personal damage is irrelevant. Do not build these stats.
- **SPD Targets:**
    - **120:** Minimum to activate Fleet of the Ageless.
    - **134:** First major breakpoint. Grants an extra action on the first cycle of Memory of Chaos, generating crucial skill points and energy.
    - **143:** Second major breakpoint for increased action frequency.
- **HP:** **Aim for 8,000 - 10,000+ HP.** This is her most important stat for survivability and buff potency.
- **DEF:** **Aim for 1,200 - 1,500 DEF.** Achieved through substats, Longevous Disciple 4pc, and potentially her light cone.
- **Energy Regeneration Rate:** **Aim for 119.4% (ERR Rope) or 124.4% (ERR Rope + Penacony set)** to ensure a 3-turn Ultimate rotation.
- **Effect RES:** **Aim for ≥30%**, ideally 50% to activate Broken Keel. Fu Xuan must avoid being crowd-controlled.

## H2: Gameplay Tips & Rotation
- **Skill Rotation:**
    1.  **Turn 1:** Use **Skill** to activate Matrix of Prescience. This is your highest priority.
    2.  **Subsequent Turns:** Maintain the Matrix. Use **Basic Attack** to generate skill points and energy unless you need to refresh the Matrix (it lasts 3 turns) or your HP is dangerously low and your Ultimate isn't ready.
    3.  **Ultimate Usage:** Use **Ultimate** primarily for its team heal and Emergency energy fill. Best used after multiple allies have taken damage to maximize the heal and generate talent stacks. Also use it to break Quantum-weak enemies.
- **Key Combos:**
    - **Pre-battle Technique:** Use her Technique to enter battle with a free stack of Formidable Fixation and 20 energy, giving you a head start on survivability and Ultimate charge.
    - **Energy Funneling:** When paired with a battery like [Tingyun](link-to-tingyun-guide) or [HuoHuo](link-to-huohuo-guide), prioritize having them funnel energy to Fu Xuan to guarantee Matrix uptime and emergency heals.
- **Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
    - **Forgetting to refresh the Matrix.** If it expires, your team suddenly takes full damage. Track its duration.
    - **Building CRIT on her.** It's a waste of resources. Her damage is not her role.
    - **Ignoring SPD.** A slow Fu Xuan generates fewer skill points, less energy, and can't apply her Matrix fast enough in changing fight conditions.
    - **Using Ultimate at full HP.** Hold it for when the team actually needs the heal, unless you need to break toughness immediately.

## H2: Investment Priority
- **E0 Viable?:** **Absolutely.** Fu Xuan is one of the most complete and powerful characters in the game at E0. She defines the meta for survival-based team compositions. You do not need any eidolons for her to be exceptionally effective.
- **Key Eidolons:**
    - **E1:** Increases the CRIT DMG buff from 10% to 20% of her HP (up to a cap). A pure damage boost for your carry, nice but not necessary.
    - **E2:** Grants the team a one-time resurrection (50% HP) when Fu Xuan uses her Ultimate. This is an incredibly powerful safety net for difficult content. The biggest Eidolon upgrade.
    - **E6:** Massively increases her own damage and toughness damage dealt, making her a viable sub-DPS. For min-maxers only.
- **Light Cone Priority:** E0 Fu Xuan > 5★ Signature LC > E2 Fu Xuan > Other 5★ LCs > E1 Fu Xuan. Getting her to E0 first is the priority. Her signature light cone is a major upgrade, but she functions perfectly well with 4★ options like **We Are Wildfire**.

## H2: FAQ

**1. What is the best build for Fu Xuan in HSR?**
The best Fu Xuan build focuses entirely on her role as a mitigation tank. Use the **Longevous Disciple 4pc** relic set with **Fleet of the Ageless** planar ornaments. Main stats should be **HP% Body, SPD Feet, HP% Sphere, and Energy Regeneration Rate Rope**. Prioritize SPD and HP% in substats.

**2. What relics should I use for Fu Xuan?**
The best relics are the **4pc Longevous Disciple** set for its HP and conditional DEF boost. For planar ornaments, **Fleet of the Ageless** provides the best mix of HP and team utility. **Broken Keel** is a strong offensive alternative if you can meet the Effect RES requirement.

**3. What is Fu Xuan's best light cone?**
Her signature 5★ light cone, **She Already Shut Her Eyes**, is her BiS as it provides HP, DEF, and a powerful team heal on her Ultimate. Among 4★ options, **We Are Wildfire** is the best, offering a valuable HP boost
