# H1: 符玄 Build Guide — Best Relics, Light Cones & Teams | GameBeliever

## H2: 符玄 Build Overview

**Role:** Tank / Support (Preservation Path)  
**Element:** Quantum  
**Faction:** Divination Commission  

Fu Xuan is not your traditional Preservation tank. Instead of providing shields, she fundamentally alters team survivability by using her **Skill, Matrix of Prescience**, to redirect a significant portion of all damage taken by her allies onto herself. This unique mechanic, combined with her Talent that increases team-wide CRIT Rate and her Ultimate that deals Quantum damage and restores HP, makes her one of the most powerful and versatile defensive units in Honkai: Star Rail. Her role is that of a "damage-mitigation engine" and a team-wide buffer.

**Why This Build Works:**  
The definitive Fu Xuan build centers on maximizing her personal survivability and effect resistance while amplifying her team support capabilities. Her Skill's damage redirection means she must survive hits meant for the entire team. Therefore, stacking HP and DEF is paramount. However, her Ultimate restores HP based on her max HP, creating a synergistic loop. Her A6 trace converts her DEF into additional CRIT DMG for the team via her Skill, incentivizing a hybrid build that values both survivability stats and some offensive stats. This build ensures she can tank lethal damage, keep her team alive, and provide a substantial CRIT buff, making every other damage dealer on your team more effective.

**Stats to Prioritize:**  
1.  **HP% / Flat HP:** The core of her survivability and her Ultimate's healing potency.
2.  **DEF%:** Enhances her personal tanking ability and, post-A6, directly converts to team CRIT DMG.
3.  **Effect RES:** Prevents her from being crowd-controlled, which is fatal as it would disrupt her Matrix and leave the team vulnerable.
4.  **SPD:** Allows her to take turns more frequently to reapply her Skill or use her Ultimate.
5.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG:** Secondary priorities to increase her own Ultimate damage, scaling with her A6 trace.

## H2: Best Relics for 符玄

### H3: Best 4pc Set
**Guard of Wuthering Snow (4pc)**  
This is the undisputed best-in-slot set for Fu Xuan. The 2-piece set grants a flat 8% Damage Reduction, which directly synergizes with her role of taking hits for the team. The 4-piece set bonus reduces the DMG taken when her HP is 50% or lower by a further 15%. Since Fu Xuan will often be below half health as she soaks damage, this set provides massive, consistent damage mitigation that far outweighs other options.

### H3: Alternative Sets
While the 4pc Guard set is ideal, here are viable 2pc + 2pc combinations if you haven't farmed a perfect 4-piece set:
*   **2pc Guard of Wuthering Snow + 2pc Longevous Disciple:** The most balanced hybrid option. It provides 8% DMG Reduction and 12% max HP, boosting both her survivability and Ultimate healing.
*   **2pc Guard of Wuthering Snow + 2pc Knight of Purity Palace:** Offers 8% DMG Reduction and a 15% DEF increase. Excellent for pushing her DEF higher to maximize the A6 trace conversion to team CRIT DMG.
*   **When to use:** Use these combinations while farming for the 4pc Guard set or if you have exceptionally high-stat relics from the alternative sets.

### H3: Best Planar Ornaments
*   **Best-in-Slot: Fleet of the Ageless (2pc)**  
    The 12% max HP boost is universally valuable for Fu Xuan. The 4-piece set effect increases all team members' ATK by 8% when her SPD reaches 120, a very achievable breakpoint. This adds a layer of offensive support to her defensive kit.
*   **Alternative Options:**
    *   **Sprightly Vonwacq (2pc):** Grants 5% Energy Regeneration Rate and advances her action forward by 40% if her SPD is ≥120. This is fantastic for ensuring she can use her Ultimate or reapply her Skill at critical moments.
    *   **Belobog of the Architects (2pc):** Provides a 15% DEF boost when her Effect RES is ≥50%. A pure defensive option if you struggle to hit other breakpoints.

### H3: Recommended Main Stats
*   **Body:** **HP%** is the primary choice for maximum survivability and healing. **DEF%** is a viable alternative if your DEF is low, as it also contributes to the A6 trace.
*   **Feet:** **SPD** is non-negotiable to ensure she takes turns frequently. **HP%** can be considered only if you already have a very high SPD from substats.
*   **Planar Sphere:** **HP%** or **DEF%**. Both are excellent; choose the one where you have better substats. HP% generally has a slight edge due to the synergy with her Ultimate.
*   **Link Rope:** **Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR)** is the best main stat. It significantly reduces the number of turns needed to cast her Ultimate, which is her primary source of emergency team healing. **HP%** can be used if you cannot secure an ERR rope.

### H3: Substats Priority
Priority order (from most to least important):
1.  **SPD:** Aim for at least **120** to activate Fleet of the Ageless or Vonwacq effects. A higher target is **134** for an extra action in the first cycle of Memory of Chaos.
2.  **HP% / DEF%:** Both are critical. Look for pieces with multiple rolls into these stats.
3.  **Effect RES:** Aim for a total of **~50%** (including traces and substats) to resist most debuffs.
4.  **Flat HP:** A useful bonus to her survivability.
5.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG:** Acceptable, especially on the Head and Hands pieces, to boost her personal Ultimate damage.

## H2: Stats Breakpoints & Targets

*   **CRIT Rate Target:** Not a primary goal for her personal build, but having some (e.g., 20-30%) will increase her Ultimate damage.
*   **CRIT DMG Target:** Similarly, not a main goal, but scales with her A6 trace indirectly.
*   **SPD Breakpoints:**
    *   **120:** Minimum target to activate Fleet of the Ageless and Sprightly Vonwacq.
    *   **134:** Important breakpoint for Memory of Chaos to gain an extra action in Cycle 1.
    *   **143:** Another common breakpoint for turn economy.
*   **ATK / EHR / ERR Targets:**
    *   **ATK:** Irrelevant for Fu Xuan. Ignore this stat.
    *   **EHR:** Not required. Her Ultimate's Entanglement application is a bonus, not the focus.
    *   **ERR:** With an ERR Rope, she needs ~106.7% ERR (base + rope) to use her Ultimate every 3 skill rotations. Higher ERR allows for even faster cycling.

## H2: Best Team Compositions

Fu Xuan's team-wide buffs and damage mitigation make her a universal slot in almost any team. Her Matrix is active as long as she has sufficient energy, which an ERR build helps maintain.

### H3: Premium Team
*   **Team Members:** **Fu Xuan (Tank/Support), Silver Wolf (Debuffer), Seele (Main DPS), Tingyun (Support)**
*   **Why it works:** This is a top-tier Mono-Quantum team. Silver Wolf guarantees Weakness implant, Seele is a hyper-carry who benefits immensely from Fu Xuan's CRIT Rate buff, and Tingyun provides ATK buffs and energy to Seele. Fu Xuan keeps this high-damage, somewhat fragile composition completely safe.

### H3: F2P Team
*   **Team Members:** **Fu Xuan (Tank/Support), Asta (Support), Sushang (Main DPS), Natasha (Healer/Insurance)**
*   **How to play:** Asta provides universal SPD and ATK buffs. Sushang is a strong, accessible Hunt DPS. Natasha serves as a safety-net healer, though Fu Xuan alone is often enough. This team is robust, easy to play, and covers all bases for early-to-mid game content.

### H3: Alternative Teams
*   **Hypercarry Support:** Replace a shielder/defensive support in any hypercarry team (e.g., Dan Heng IL, Jing Yuan) with Fu Xuan. Her CRIT buff and tanking are a massive upgrade.
*   **Dual Carry:** Place her alongside another moderately durable DPS (e.g., Blade, Clara) who can benefit from her buffs while sharing the damage-taking load.
*   **Break Effect Team:** Her ability to survive allows your Break Effect units (e.g., Serval, Sampo) to focus entirely on breaking toughness bars without fear of dying.

## H2: Gameplay Tips & Rotation

**Skill Rotation:**  
1.  **Turn 1:** Use **Skill (Matrix of Prescience)**. This is your highest priority to activate damage redirection and the CRIT buff.
2.  **Subsequent Turns:** Monitor the Matrix buff duration (3 turns). If it's about to expire or your energy is full, consider using your **Ultimate (Woes of Many Morphed into One)**. The Ultimate deals AoE Quantum damage, applies Entanglement, and, most importantly, heals all allies based on her max HP. This is your emergency "oh no" button.
3.  **Basic Attacks:** Use Basic Attacks to generate Skill Points and Energy when your Matrix is still active and your Ultimate is not ready.
4.  **Key Combo:** **Skill (apply Matrix) -> Basic Attack x2 -> Ultimate (reapply Matrix & heal)**. This is a common loop.

**Key Combos:**  
*   **Emergency Heal:** If a teammate is critically low and the enemy is about to attack, using Fu Xuan's Ultimate can immediately heal them to safety via her Talent's effect.
*   **Preemptive Ultimate:** Use her Ultimate just before her Skill buff expires to refresh both the Matrix and the team's HP simultaneously.

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**  
1.  **Forgetting to Reapply Matrix:** If the Matrix expires, your team becomes extremely vulnerable. Always track its duration.
2.  **Using Ultimate at Full HP:** Her Ultimate's healing is based on her max HP, not missing HP. It's often better to use it when the team is damaged or to pre-emptively reapply the Matrix, not just for damage.
3.  **Ignoring DEF:** While HP is king, neglecting DEF (especially with the A6 trace) leaves significant team CRIT DMG on the table.
4.  **Building Attack:** Fu Xuan does not scale with ATK. Building it is a waste of stats.

## H2: Investment Priority

*   **E0 Viable?:** **Absolutely.** Fu Xuan is fully functional and one of the best tanks in the game at E0. Her core mechanics—the Matrix, the CRIT buff, and the damage redirection—are all present in her base kit.
*   **Key Eidolons:**
    *   **E1 (The Weight of Truth):** Increases her own HP by 18%. A simple, powerful survivability boost.
    *   **E2 (The Vision of Foresight):** When she uses her Ultimate, all allies' CRIT Rate is increased by 12% for 2 turns. This is a massive DPS increase for the team, turning her into a potent offensive support.
    *   **E6 (The Truth of All):** Makes her Talent's CRIT Rate buff permanent once the Matrix is activated and increases the CRIT Rate buff by an additional 15%. This is a game-changing quality-of-life and power increase, allowing her to maintain a 30%+ CRIT Rate buff on the team effortlessly.
*   **Light Cone Priority:** For most players, **E0 is the top priority**, followed by obtaining a good 4★ or 5★ Light Cone (like She Already Shut Her Eyes if you can), and then saving for key Eidolons like **E2** or **E6** for long-term investment.

## H2: FAQ

1.  **What is the best build for Fu Xuan in HSR?**
    The best build focuses on survivability and support. Use the 4pc **Guard of Wuthering Snow** set with main stats **HP%/SPD/HP% or DEF%/ERR Rope**. Prioritize SPD, HP%, DEF%, and Effect RES substats.

2.  **What relics should I use for Fu Xuan?**
    Her best relic set is **Guard of Wuthering Snow (4pc)**. For planar ornaments, **Fleet of the Ageless (2pc)** is best-in-slot for team support. **Sprightly Vonwacq (2pc)** is an excellent alternative for turn control.

3.  **What is Fu Xuan's best light cone?**
    Her signature 5★, **She Already Shut Her Eyes**, is her undisputed best-in-slot. For 4★ options, **Day One of My New Life** is fantastic. As a free-to-play player, **We Are Wildfire** from the Herta Store is exceptional.

4.  **What team is best for Fu Xuan?**
    Fu Xuan is flexible. A premium **Mono-Quantum team** with **Silver Wolf, Seele, and Tingyun** is top-tier. She also fits perfectly into any **hypercarry team** (e.g., with Dan Heng IL or Jing Yuan) as the defensive support.

5.  **Is Fu Xuan good in Honkai Star Rail?**
    Yes, Fu Xuan is considered one of the best and most versatile characters in the game. She provides unparalleled team survivability, a potent CRIT Rate buff, and some personal damage, making her a universal upgrade for almost any team composition.
