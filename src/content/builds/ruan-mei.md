Ruan Mei is a game-changing Harmony Support who debuted in version 1.6. As a 5★ Ice character on the Path of Harmony, she doesn't just buff; she fundamentally alters the pace and power ceiling of any team she joins. ## Ruan Mei Build Overview

**Role & Playstyle:** Ruan Mei is a premier offensive support whose kit revolves around empowering her allies to deal more damage and break enemy weaknesses faster. Her unique mechanics provide a permanent, high-damage buff to the entire team's Break Effect and increase the multiplier of their attacks when targeting a weakness-broken enemy. Her Ultimate creates a field that not only buffs allies but also applies a debuff that delays enemy recovery after being broken.

**Build Philosophy:** The **Ruan Mei best build** is centered around stacking Break Effect, maximizing her Skill uptime, and ensuring she can use her Ultimate consistently. While she doesn't require offensive stats like CRIT or ATK, her personal damage contribution is tied to Break Effect, making it her primary scaling stat. Speed and Energy Regeneration are secondary but crucial priorities.

**Why This Build Works:** This build maximizes Ruan Mei's core function: enabling her teammates. By focusing on Break Effect and Speed, we ensure her Skill (which provides the massive DMG boost) is always active, her Ultimate is online frequently, and her passive buffs are at their strongest. This turns her into a universal amplifier for any damage dealer, especially those who prioritize Weakness Break or operate in Dual DPS teams.

## Best Relics for Ruan Mei

### Best 4pc Set

-   **Set Recommendation: Messenger Hackerspace Traveler (4pc)**
-   **Set Bonus Explanation:**
    -   **2pc Bonus:** Increases SPD by 6%. This is foundational for meeting crucial SPD breakpoints and ensuring Ruan Mei moves frequently to refresh her Skill.
    -   **4pc Bonus:** After using Ruan Mei's Ultimate, all allies' SPD increases by 12% for 1 turn. This is an exceptional team-wide Speed boost that significantly accelerates your entire team's turn order, allowing for more actions in a cycle and better Weakness Break application. The synergy with her Ultimate-centric playstyle is perfect.

### Alternative Sets

-   **2pc + 2pc Combinations:**
    -   **2pc Messenger Hackerspace + 2pc Thief of Shooting Meteor:** The best hybrid option. Messenger provides the essential SPD, while Thief of Shooting Meteor adds 16% Break Effect. This is ideal if you cannot meet Break Effect substat goals with a 4pc Messenger set.
    -   **2pc Messenger Hackerspace + 2pc Wuthering Snow or Musketeer of Wild Wheat:** Use the second 2pc set to fill in a missing main stat (e.g., an HP% or DEF% body for survivability). This is a stepping stone before farming a complete 4pc set.

**When to Use Each:** The 4pc Messenger is universally best-in-slot. Use the 2pc+2pc combos as transitional sets while farming or if your substats on a 4pc Messenger set are exceptionally poor.

### Best Planar Ornaments

-   **Best-in-Slot: Broken Keel**
    -   **Why:** Provides 10% Effect RES for Ruan Mei (helping her resist crowd control) and a team-wide 10% CRIT DMG bonus. This secondary effect is incredibly powerful and easy to activate with Ruan Mei's naturally high Effect RES.
-   **Alternative Options:**
    -   **Penacony, Land of the Dreams:** Increases her DMG against debuffed enemies and provides 5% Energy Regeneration Rate. Excellent for boosting her personal damage and Ultimate uptime.
    -   **Sprightly Vonwacq:** The 5% ERR and the advance-forward effect when her Energy is high can help in achieving a 3-turn Ultimate rotation.

### Substats Priority

1.  **Speed (SPD):** Aim for at least 134+ for one extra action in the first cycle of Memory of Chaos. 140+ is ideal for smoother rotations.
2.  **Break Effect (BE):** Your second priority. Her major trace converts BE into a greater team DMG bonus. The target is **180% BE** to maximize her A2 trace bonus. Any BE beyond this still increases her personal damage.
3.  **Effect RES:** To activate Broken Keel and resist debuffs. Around 30% is sufficient.
4.  **HP%/DEF%:** For survivability. Avoid flat stats.

**Minimum Breakpoints:** 134 SPD (soft goal), 180% Break Effect (hard goal for trace activation).

## Stats Breakpoints & Targets

-   **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG:** Not a priority. Ruan Mei is a support, not a damage dealer. Ignore these stats.
-   **SPD:** **134** is the first major breakpoint for an extra action in cycle 1. **145** is the next tier for consistent 3-turn Ultimates with an ERR rope. Aim for the highest you can achieve without sacrificing Break Effect.
-   **Break Effect:** **180%** is the golden number to fully activate her A2 Major Trace, which increases the bonus DMG dealt to Weakness Broken enemies from 25% to 32%.
-   **Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR):** An ERR Rope is **mandatory**. This is the single most important stat for her rotation consistency.
-   **Effect RES:** ~30% to activate Broken Keel reliably.

## Gameplay Tips & Rotation

-   **Skill Rotation:** Your priority is 100% uptime on her Skill. **Use her Skill at the start of battle and whenever it expires (after 3 turns).** Her Skill grants a permanent buff to all allies' DMG and Weakness Break DMG.
-   **Ultimate Usage:** Activate her Ultimate as soon as it's ready. The field increases Weakness Break DMG and, more importantly, reduces the enemy's Toughness recovery after they are broken. This extended vulnerability window is a massive damage boost.
-   **Basic Attack vs Skill:** Only use her Basic Attack when her Skill is still active and you need to generate Skill Points for your main DPS. The goal is to use the Skill every 3 turns.
-   **Key Combos:** In a fast cycle, you can use her Ultimate, then her Skill on her next turn, ensuring maximum buff overlap. Her Technique should be used before engaging any battle, especially in Memory of Chaos, as it grants a free Skill Point.
-   **Common Mistakes:** Letting her Skill buff expire is a huge DPS loss. Forgetting to use her Ultimate on enemies about to recover from Break wastes its debuff. Neglecting Speed and ERR substats will cripple her rotation.

## FAQ

1.  **What is the best build for Ruan Mei in HSR?**
    The best **Ruan Mei build** focuses on Speed and Break Effect. Use 4pc Messenger Hackerspace relics with SPD feet, an ERR rope, and a survivability body (HP%/DEF%). Pair with Broken Keel planar ornaments. Aim for 134+ SPD and 180% Break Effect.

2.  **What relics should I use for Ruan Mei?**
    The best **Ruan Mei relics** are the 4pc Messenger Hackerspace set for the team-wide Speed buff. The best planar ornament set is Broken Keel. As a budget alternative, use 2pc Messenger Hackerspace + 2pc Thief of Shooting Meteor.

3.  **What is Ruan Mei's best light cone?**
    Her signature 5★ cone, **Past Self in the Mirror**, is her undisputed best in slot. For a 4★ alternative, **Memories of the Past** is excellent and highly recommended for non-whale players.

4.  **What team is best for Ruan Mei?**
    Ruan Mei is a universal support. Her **best teams** include Hypercarry setups with Seele, Dual DoT teams with Kafka and Black Swan, and Follow-Up Attack teams with Clara or Dr. Ratio. She elevates almost any team composition.

5.  **Is Ruan Mei good in Honkai Star Rail?**
    Yes, Ruan Mei is considered one of the most powerful and versatile characters in the game. She provides unprecedented team-wide damage amplification and Weakness Break efficiency, making her a top-tier support for all endgame content.

6.  **What does Ruan Mei scale with in HSR?**
    Ruan Mei does not scale with traditional DPS stats. Her primary scaling stat is **Break Effect**, which increases both her personal damage and the team-wide bonus she provides via her Major Traces. Speed is her other critical scaling stat for rotation consistency.

7.  **Is Ruan Mei a DPS or support?**
    Ruan Mei is a **pure support**. While she can deal decent personal damage if built with high Break Effect, her primary role is to buff her teammates' damage, break efficiency, and speed.

8.  **What is Ruan Mei's best F2P light cone?**
    The best F2P light cone for Ruan Mei is the 4★ **Memories of the Past**. If you are a brand-new player, the 3★ **Harmonious Choir** can serve as a temporary placeholder.
