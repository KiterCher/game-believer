## H2: Firefly Build Overview

**Role:** Firefly is a 5★ Fire DPS character following the **Path of Destruction**. Her kit revolves around unleashing devastating **AoE (Area of Effect)** Fire damage, primarily through her Ultimate, which hits all enemies. Her core mechanic involves managing her **Skill** to enhance her attacks and trigger powerful follow-ups, while her **Talent** passively boosts her damage based on her **ATK** and the number of enemies on the battlefield. This makes her exceptionally strong in multi-target scenarios like Memory of Chaos or Pure Fiction.

**Build Philosophy & Why This Works:** This build transforms Firefly into a **burst-oriented monster**. By stacking **ATK%**, **CRIT** stats, and leveraging her unique scaling, we maximize the damage output of her Ultimate and enhanced Skills. The priority is to achieve a high **CRIT Rate** to ensure her powerful hits land consistently, followed by a massive **CRIT DMG** multiplier. **Speed** is secondary but important for more turns and faster Ultimate cycling. This build works because it directly amplifies every component of her damage profile, from her base hits to her devastating ultimate.

**Stat Priorities:**  
**CRIT Rate = CRIT DMG > ATK% > Speed > Break Effect**

## H2: Best Relics for Firefly

### H3: Best 4pc Set: **Band of Sizzling Thunder**

*   **Why:** This is unequivocally Firefly's **Best-in-Slot (BiS)** relic set. The 2-piece bonus provides a solid **12% ATK increase**, directly boosting her primary scaling stat. The 4-piece bonus is the cornerstone of her kit: after using her Skill, her **CRIT DMG increases by 25%** for the next 2 attacks. Since her rotation heavily involves using her Skill followed by her Ultimate (which counts as an attack), this set perfectly synergizes with her burst windows, delivering a massive damage spike exactly when needed.

*   **Set Bonus Explanation:**  
    *   **2-Piece:** Increases ATK by 12%.  
    *   **4-Piece:** After using Skill, increases CRIT DMG by 25% for the next 2 attacks.

### H3: Alternative Sets: 2pc + 2pc Combinations

If you lack a good 4pc **Band of Sizzling Thunder** set, these combinations are strong transitional options:

*   **2pc Band of Sizzling Thunder + 2pc Musketeer of Wild Wheat:** A balanced option. Grants **+12% ATK** and **+6% SPD**. The speed helps reach important breakpoints, making this an excellent early-to-mid game choice.
*   **2pc Band of Sizzling Thunder + 2pc Firesmith of Lava-Forging:** A more damage-focused alternative. Provides **+12% ATK** and **+10% Fire DMG Bonus**. Use this if you have excellent CRIT substats on the Firesmith pieces and need to capitalize on her Fire Damage.

**When to Use:** Use the **2pc+2pc** combos while farming for your ideal 4pc **Band of Sizzling Thunder** set. The **Musketeer** blend is great for general progression, while the **Firesmith** mix can edge out in pure damage if your substats are superior.

### H3: Best Planar Ornaments: **Inert Salsotto**

*   **Best-in-Socket:** **Inert Salsotto**. The **8% CRIT Rate** is invaluable, and the **15% increase to Ultimate and Follow-up Attack DMG** is tailor-made for Firefly's primary damage source—her Ultimate. This is the definitive choice for maximizing her burst potential.

*   **Alternative Options:**  
    *   **Rutilant Arena:** A solid second choice if you can meet its 70% CRIT Rate requirement. It provides **8% CRIT Rate** and a **20% Basic Attack and Skill DMG increase**, which is beneficial for her Skill-focused rotation.
    *   **Space Sealing Station:** A simpler alternative that offers **12% ATK**. It's less specialized but easy to activate and provides consistent value.

### H3: Recommended Main Stats

*   **Body:** **CRIT Rate** or **CRIT DMG**. Choose whichever you need to approach your ideal ratio (see Stats Breakpoints).
*   **Feet:** **Speed** is the recommended main stat to help her reach key Speed breakpoints, allowing for more actions and faster Ultimate cycles. An **ATK%** main stat can be considered for pure damage in highly optimized teams where external Speed buffs are guaranteed.
*   **Planar Sphere:** **Fire DMG Bonus**. This is a multiplier on all her damage and is superior to ATK%.
*   **Link Rope:** **ATK%**. Further amplifies her scaling. An **Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR)** rope can be considered in specific team compositions to ensure 100% Ultimate uptime, but ATK% is generally preferred for maximum damage.

### H3: Substats Priority

The substats you hunt for follow a clear hierarchy:

1.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG** (Maintain a 1:2 ratio as close as possible)
2.  **ATK%**
3.  **Speed** (To reach breakpoints)
4.  **Break Effect** (Nice to have, but not a priority)

**Minimum Breakpoints:** Aim for at least **2-3 good rolls** (4-6 substat value) into CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG on each relic piece.

## H2: Stats Breakpoints & Targets

Aiming for these stat values at Level 80 will ensure Firefly performs at her peak:

*   **CRIT Rate:** **70%+**. This is the crucial breakpoint to activate the **Rutilant Arena** planar ornament bonus and ensure her CRITs are frequent. With good substats and a CRIT Rate body, this is very achievable.
*   **CRIT DMG:** **140%+**. The goal is a **1:2 ratio with CRIT Rate**. So if you have 70% CRIT Rate, aim for 140% CRIT DMG. This is a realistic target with a 4pc **Band of Sizzling Thunder** set active.
*   **Speed:** **115-120**. This is a comfortable range that allows for 3 actions within the first two cycles of battle, aligning with common support character Speed Tuning. Reaching **134** is the "golden" breakpoint for 4 actions in the first two cycles but requires significant investment.
*   **ATK (in-combat):** **3000+**. This is a general guideline. Her true damage potential is measured more by her CRIT stats and DMG bonuses (like Fire DMG), but a high ATK base is necessary.
*   **Break Effect:** Not a primary target, but **50%+** is a nice bonus if you get it from substats.

## H2: Best Team Compositions

Firefly's teams focus on amplifying her Fire damage, providing debuffs, and ensuring she has enough Energy and survivability to unleash her Ultimate.

### H3: Premium Team: Firefly / **Ruan Mei** / **Fu Xuan** / **Topaz and Numby**

*   **Team Members:** Firefly (Main DPS), Ruan Mei (Harmony Support), Fu Xuan (Preservation/CRIT Buffer), Topaz and Numby (Sub-DPS/Debuffer).
*   **Why it Works:** This is a hypercarry team built for maximum damage. **Ruan Mei** provides all-type RES PEN, DMG%, and Weakness Break Efficiency, which is god-tier for Firefly. **Fu Xuan** offers team-wide CRIT Rate, survivability, and can carry the **Trend of the Universal Market** light cone to generate extra Energy when hit. **Topaz** applies a Fire vulnerability debuff, amplifying Firefly's damage further, while contributing her own damage. This team excels in destroying elite enemies and bosses weak to Fire.

### H3: F2P Team: Firefly / **Bronya** / **Tingyun** / **Fire Trailblazer**

*   **Team Members:** Firefly, Bronya (or Asta), Tingyun, Fire Trailblazer (or March 7th/Gepard).
*   **Budget Options & How to Play:** **Tingyun** is the core support here, providing ATK buffs, DMG%, and crucial Energy restoration to Fuel Firefly's Ultimate. **Bronya** can advance Firefly's action forward for immediate skill use, while **Asta** is a fantastic alternative, offering massive SPD buffs and a Fire DMG% buff for the team. **Fire Trailblazer** provides a shield and Taunt to protect Firefly while generating extra Skill Points. The goal is to cycle Firefly's Ultimate as often as possible using Tingyun's help.

### H3: Alternative Teams: **DoT/Debuff Hybrid**

*   **Team Members:** Firefly / **Black Swan** / **Kafka** / **Ruan Mei**.
*   **Why it Works:** An experimental but potent composition. While not a traditional DoT team, **Black Swan** and **Kafka** apply potent DoTs that benefit from Ruan Mei's All-Type RES PEN. Firefly acts as the primary burst DPS to clear waves and finish off enemies weakened by the DoTs. This team has exceptional coverage against multiple enemy types.

## H2: Gameplay Tips & Rotation

**Skill Rotation Guide:**  
The core of Firefly's gameplay is generating and spending Energy for her Ultimate.

1.  **Basic Attack/Enhanced Basic:** Use this when your Skill is not needed or you are saving Energy.
2.  **Skill:** Use this to enhance your next attack and gain Energy. The enhanced state is key for your damage window.
3.  **Ultimate:** Activate **immediately** after using your Skill to benefit from the **Band of Sizzling Thunder** 4-piece CRIT DMG buff. This is your main damage source.
4.  **Talent Management:** In AoE fights, try to position so your attacks hit multiple targets, as her Talent's damage bonus scales with the number of enemies on the field.

**Key Combos:**
*   **Standard Burst:** Skill -> Ultimate -> Enhanced Basic Attack. This is your bread and butter.
*   **With Bronya:** Skill -> Bronya's Skill (advance Firefly) -> Ultimate -> Enhanced Basic Attack.

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
*   **Not activating Ultimate after Skill:** You lose the massive CRIT DMG buff from your relic set.
*   **Poor Energy Management:** Using your Ultimate immediately off cooldown, even if it means you can't use your Skill first. Always prioritize Skill -> Ultimate for the buff.
*   **Ignoring Speed:** Building Firefly with no Speed makes her clunky and reliant on external Speed buffs to be effective.

## H2: Investment Priority

*   **E0 Viable?** **Absolutely.** Firefly is a complete and powerful character at Eidolon 0 (E0). All Eidolons are upgrades, but none are mandatory for her to function as a top-tier DPS.
*   **Key Eidolons:** **E1** is a significant quality-of-life and damage increase, often enhancing her Skill or Talent. **E2** typically provides another major damage multiplier or utility boost. **E6** is a transformative power spike. For most players, aiming for **E0 or E1** is the recommended stopping point.

It should be your top priority after securing her E0, unless you have another excellent 5★ Destruction cone. **E1** and her **Signature LC** are close in priority—choose based on your luck and resource stockpile.
