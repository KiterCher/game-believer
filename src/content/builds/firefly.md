# Firefly Build Guide — Best Relics, Light Cones & Teams | GameBeliever

## Best Firefly Build Overview

**Role:** Break DPS | **Element:** Fire | **Path:** Destruction

Firefly is a high-risk, high-reward hypercarry who operates on a unique health-consumption mechanic and excels in Breaking enemy Weaknesses. The **best Firefly build** revolves around maximizing her Break Effect, Attack, and Speed while leveraging her Enhanced State to deal massive AoE Fire damage. Her kit rewards aggressive play and careful HP management.

## Best Relics for Firefly

Firefly's relic choices are dictated by her scaling on Break Effect and her need for Speed to act frequently in her Enhanced State.

### Primary Set (4pc)
*   **Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge:** This is the undisputed **best relic set for Firefly**. Its 2-piece bonus provides a 16% Break Effect increase. The 4-piece bonus grants an additional Break Effect boost equal to 25% of the wearer's DEF when Break Effect exceeds 150%, pushing her to devastating Break thresholds. This set perfectly synergizes with her kit.

### Alternative Sets (2pc+2pc)
*   **2pc Fire DMG + 2pc Break Effect:** Combine the **Firesmith of Lava-Forging** (10% Fire DMG) and **Talia: Kingdom of Banditry** (16% Break Effect) planar ornaments for a balanced boost to her elemental damage and Break scaling. A strong option if you lack a full 4-piece Iron Cavalry.
*   **2pc Messenger Traversing Hackerspace + 2pc Thief of Shooting Meteor:** The 2-piece Hackerspace provides a crucial 6% Speed boost to help hit important SPD breakpoints, while the Thief set offers Break Effect and energy regeneration on Weakness Break, aiding Ultimate uptime.

### Planar Ornaments
*   **Talia: Kingdom of Banditry (4pc):** The premier choice for maximizing Firefly's damage output. It grants a massive 20% Break Effect increase if her Speed is 145 or higher after battle start. This is a core part of the **best Firefly relics** setup.
*   **Firmament Frontline: Glamath (2pc):** A viable alternative that provides 15% Break Effect, making it easier to reach the Iron Cavalry 4-piece bonus threshold.

For a deeper dive into relic farming strategies and main stat priorities, check our detailed [Relic Guide](/relics/iron-cavalry-set).

## Best Light Cones for Firefly

As a 5-star Destruction unit, Firefly has powerful signature and alternative options.

### Best-in-Slot Light Cone
*   **Whereabouts Should Dreams Rest (5-star):** Firefly's signature **Light Cone is, without question, the best for Firefly**. It provides a colossal 28% increase in Break Effect at Superimposition 1. Its unique secondary effect grants "Searing Sting," which, when the wearer's turn begins, increases their ATK by 20% for 2 turns if their current HP is 80% or less. This perfectly complements her HP-consuming playstyle and Break-focused kit.

### F2P / Alternative Options
*   **Indelible Promise (4-star, Herta Store):** A fantastic F2P **Firefly Light Cone** option. It boosts Break Effect by 24% at S5 and increases CRIT DMG against debuffed enemies, which is easily maintained with a team that applies debuffs.
*   **An Inevitable Tax (5-star, Standard Banner):** Provides a generic but powerful 24% ATK boost and a conditional 18% CRIT Rate increase when using an Ultimate. A strong stat stick.
*   **Flames Afar (4-star, Gacha):** A niche but effective option that increases ATK by 20% and grants a shield when HP drops below 50%, helping to sustain her during aggressive HP consumption.

## Recommended Stats & Substats

### Main Stats
*   **Body:** **ATK%** is the primary choice. Firefly's damage scales directly from ATK, and her major traces do not provide innate Crit buffs.
*   **Feet:** **Speed (SPD)**. Reaching the **145 SPD breakpoint is non-negotiable** to activate the Talia planar ornament set and ensure frequent turns in her Enhanced State.
*   **Planar Sphere:** **Fire DMG Boost**. Provides a direct multiplier to her skill and ultimate damage.
*   **Link Rope:** **Break Effect**. This is the most important offensive stat after SPD, as her talent and scaling heavily incentivize stacking it.

### Substats Priority Order
1.  **Break Effect** (Until you hit a high threshold, e.g., 200%+)
2.  **Speed** (To reach the 145 breakpoint)
3.  **ATK%**
4.  **HP% / DEF%** (For survivability, as she consumes her own HP)

### Key Stat Thresholds
*   **145 SPD:** Mandatory for the Talia set bonus.
*   **150%+ Break Effect:** This is the activation threshold for the 4-piece Iron Cavalry set bonus. Aim to exceed this with substats and Light Cone bonuses.
