## Gallagher Build Overview

**Role:** Healer / Debuffer Support  
**Element:** Fire  
**Path:** Abundance  
**Rarity:** 4★  
**Base SPD:** 100  

Gallagher is a unique hybrid healer who scales off **ATK** rather than the traditional HP scaling most Abundance characters follow. His kit revolves around applying **Besotted** (a unique debuff) to enemies, which causes allies attacking those enemies to receive healing. This offensive healing philosophy makes him an exceptional fit for aggressive team compositions that want to deal damage while sustaining.

### Why This Build Works

Unlike conventional healers like Bailu or Natasha who scale primarily with HP and DEF, Gallagher's healing output is tied to his **ATK stat** and his ability to consistently apply his Besotted debuff. This means building him for ATK simultaneously increases his healing throughput and his personal damage — a rare win-win that makes him incredibly efficient in terms of stat investment. His Ultimate also provides AoE Fire damage and applies Besotted to all enemies hit, giving him strong team-wide utility. The [Abundance Path characters guide](https://gamebeliever.com/abundance-path-guide) breaks down how healers function within the Path system.

### What Stats to Prioritize

The core stat priority for Gallagher is:

1. **ATK%** — Directly scales his healing and damage
2. **SPD** — More turns = more Skill usage = more healing and debuff uptime
3. **Effect Hit Rate (EHR)** — Ensures Besotted lands on high-RES enemies
4. **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG** — For personal damage contribution
5. **Break Effect** — Synergizes with Fire Break application

## Best Relics for Gallagher

### Best 4pc Set

**4pc Thief of Shooting Meteor** is the **best-in-slot** set for Gallagher. This set provides:

- **2pc Bonus:** Increases Break Effect by 16%.
- **4pc Bonus:** When the wearer inflicts Break on an enemy, regenerates 3 Energy and deals additional break-adjacent damage.

Since Gallagher applies Fire Break consistently through his attacks and his Fire implant mechanic, this set maximizes his Break Effect scaling while providing extra energy regeneration to keep his Ultimate uptime high. The Break Effect bonus also directly synergizes with the Fire Break DoT that Gallagher applies, creating a snowball of sustained damage.

**Runner-up: 4pc Thief of Shooting Meteor** remains the single best choice. There is no realistic 4pc alternative that competes. If you're farming the [Relic domain guide](https://gamebeliever.com/relic-farming-guide), prioritize this set.

### Alternative Sets

**2pc Thief of Shooting Meteor + 2pc Musketeer of Wild Wheat**

- **When to use:** When you don't have a complete 4pc Thief set with good substats. The 2pc Break Effect (16%) combined with 2pc ATK% (12%) is a solid compromise that boosts both his healing and Break contribution.

**2pc Musketeer of Wild Wheat + 2pc Messenger Traversing Hackerspace**

- **When to use:** Early-to-mid game when you need survivability. The ATK% and SPD bonus helps Gallagher cycle turns faster and maintain consistent healing output.

### Best Planar Ornaments

**Best-in-Slot: Penacony, Land of the Dreams**
- **2pc Bonus:** Increases Energy Regeneration Rate by 5%. Additionally, increases DMG dealt by all other allies that share the same Path as the wearer by 10%.
- Since Gallagher is a 4-star Abundance character, the 10% DMG bonus to Path-sharing allies is niche but the ERR is universally valuable. This set ensures he can cycle his Ultimate more frequently, maintaining Besotted uptime on all enemies.

**Alternative: Fleet of the Ageless**
- Increases Max HP by 12%. When the wearer's SPD reaches 120 or higher, all allies' ATK increases by 8%.
- A solid option if you can reach 120 SPD. The team-wide ATK buff synergizes well with damage-focused teams.

**Alternative: Sprightly Vonwacq**
- Increases Energy Regeneration Rate by 5%. When the wearer's SPD reaches 120 or higher, advances their action by 40% at the start of battle.
- Useful for ensuring Gallagher can apply Besotted on turn one for aggressive openers. Check our [Planar Ornament ranking](https://gamebeliever.com/planar-ornament-tier-list) for more options.

## Gameplay Tips & Rotation

### Skill Rotation

1. **Start of Battle:** Use Technique if available to apply initial debuffs/Buffs
2. **Ultimate Priority:** Cast Ultimate as soon as it's available — it applies Besotted to ALL enemies, which is your most important contribution
3. **Basic Attacks on debuffed enemies:** When enemies have Besotted, your Basic Attacks heal allies — use this to sustain without spending Skill Points
4. **Skill usage:** Use Skill when Besotted has fallen off and you need to reapply healing or when burst healing is needed

### Key Combos

- **Ultimate → Enhanced Basic Attack:** Gallagher's Ultimate enhances his next Basic Attack, dealing additional Fire damage. Always try to use your Enhanced Basic immediately after Ultimate for maximum damage.
- **Debuff Stacking:** If using in an Acheron team, remember that each action Gallagher takes that applies a debuff contributes to Acheron's Ultimate stack.

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Overusing Skill Points** — Gallagher's Basic Attacks are powerful and often sufficient for healing. Don't waste SP when Basic Attacks will do.
2. **Ignoring EHR** — Without enough EHR, Besotted won't land on bosses, crippling your healing output.
3. **Forgetting Enhanced Basic** — After Ultimate, your next Basic is enhanced. Never waste it on a non-attack action.
4. **Building HP% instead of ATK%** — This is the most common mistake. Gallagher heals with ATK, not HP.

## FAQ

### 1. What is the best build for Gallagher in HSR?

The best Gallagher build focuses on **ATK% body, SPD boots, ATK% or HP% sphere, and ERR rope** with 4pc Thief of Shooting Meteor relics. Prioritize SPD and ATK% substats, with EHR as a secondary goal to ensure Besotted debuff lands consistently.

### 2. What relics should I use for Gallagher?

**4pc Thief of Shooting Meteor** is his best-in-slot set due to Break Effect scaling and energy regeneration. For Planar Ornaments, **Penacony, Land of the Dreams** provides the best ERR utility, while **Fleet of the Ageless** offers a team-wide ATK buff if you can reach 120 SPD.

### 3. What is Gallagher's best light cone?

**Quid Pro Quo** is Gallagher's best-in-slot 5★ light cone. For 4★ options, **What Is Real?** is widely considered his best budget choice, offering strong ATK% scaling that synergizes with his unique healing mechanics.

### 4. What team is best for Gallagher?

Gallagher's best premium team is **Acheron + Pela + Silver Wolf + Gallagher**, where his debuff application fuels Acheron's Ultimate. For Break-focused teams, **Firefly + Ruan Mei + Harmony Trailblazer + Gallagher** is an incredibly powerful meta composition.

### 5. Is Gallagher good in Honkai Star Rail?

Gallagher is considered one of the **best 4-star characters in HSR**. His unique ATK-scaling healing, debuff application, and Break Effect synergy make him competitive with many 5-star sustain options. He's particularly valuable in teams that benefit from additional debuffs.

### 6. What does Gallagher scale with in HSR?

Unlike most healers who scale with HP, Gallagher scales primarily with **ATK**. His healing output and personal damage both increase with ATK, making ATK% his most important stat alongside SPD and Effect Hit Rate.

### 7. Is Gallagher a DPS or support?

Gallagher is a **hybrid healer/support**. While he follows the Abundance (healer) Path, his kit deals meaningful damage and applies debuffs that amplify team damage. He's best classified as an offensive sustain who contributes to both survival and offense simultaneously.

### 8. What is Gallagher's best F2P light cone?

**Nexus of Consciousness** from the event or **Cornucopia** (3★) are the best free-to-play options. Both provide functional stat boosts that support his ATK-scaling healing until you can acquire What Is Real? or a 5★ alternative.
