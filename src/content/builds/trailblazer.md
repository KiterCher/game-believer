# 开拓者 Build Guide — Best Relics, Light Cones & Teams | GameBeliever

## 开拓者 Build Overview

**Role:** Main DPS  
**Element:** Physical  
**Path:** Destruction  

Trailblazer, the protagonist of Honkai: Star Rail, is a surprisingly potent 4-star Physical DPS who shines in early to mid-game content. As a Destruction character, her kit is designed for dealing sustained, often AoE, damage while leveraging the powerful Bleed DoT (Damage over Time). Her playstyle revolves around consistent pressure, with her Ultimate providing a significant burst of AoE Physical damage.

**Why This Build Works:** This guide outlines a build focused on maximizing Trailblazer's raw damage output and reliability. The philosophy is to transform her from a serviceable starter unit into a true powerhouse who can clear content efficiently. We achieve this by stacking CRIT stats to ensure her attacks and Ultimate hit hard, while also incorporating sufficient Effect Hit Rate (EHR) to consistently apply her core mechanic: **Bleed**. The Physical Element and Destruction Path allow her to break Physical-weak enemies quickly, further amplifying her damage. Prioritizing **CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, and EHR** will yield the best results, making her a formidable main carry for any Physical-focused team composition.

## Best Relics for Trailblazer

### Best 4pc Set
**4pc Physical DMG Set: Streetwise Boxing**
This is the undisputed Best-in-Slot (BiS) for a pure damage-focused Trailblazer. The 2-piece bonus provides a solid +10% Physical DMG boost, directly scaling her Basic Attacks, Skill, and Ultimate. The 4-piece bonus is where its true power lies: it increases CRIT Rate by 25% for each time the wearer attacks an enemy, stacking up to 5 times. For a character like Trailblazer who will be attacking every turn, maintaining a high stack is easy, providing a massive, permanent CRIT Rate buff that simplifies stat building immensely.

### Alternative Sets
If you're farming and haven't obtained a complete Streetwise Boxing set, strong alternatives exist:
- **2pc Streetwise Boxing + 2pc Musketeer of Wild Wheat:** The most straightforward hybrid. You get the +10% Physical DMG from Boxing and a universal +12% ATK from Musketeer. This is an excellent, easy-to-build placeholder.
- **2pc Musketeer of Wild Wheat + 2pc Guard of Wuthering Snow:** A more defensive option for survivability. You gain +12% ATK and a shield when HP drops below 50%. Use this if you're struggling to keep Trailblazer alive in challenging content before you have proper team sustain.

**When to use each:** Use the full 4pc Streetwise Boxing for all endgame content like Memory of Chaos. The 2pc + 2pc combos are perfect for story mode, Simulated Universe, and early Memory of Chaos floors.

### Best Planar Ornaments
- **Best-in-Slot: Inert Salsotto**  
  This set is tailor-made for DPS like Trailblazer. The +8% CRIT Rate is a great foundation, and the +15% DMG boost to both Ultimate and follow-up attacks (if applicable) directly amplifies her strongest tool. It's the most efficient way to stack offensive power.
- **Alternative Option: Space Sealing Station**  
  If you find yourself lacking ATK% on substats, this set provides a flat +24% ATK, which is always a valuable boost for increasing raw damage numbers. It's simpler and very effective.
- **Niche Option: Celestial Differentiator**  
  If you are having trouble reaching the 70% CRIT Rate breakpoint (especially if not using the 4pc Streetwise Boxing), this set can help by giving CRIT Rate based on your SPD, potentially freeing up substat rolls.

### Recommended Main Stats
- **Body:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (Choose whichever helps you reach the ideal 1:2 ratio. Often CRIT Rate is chosen early on to ensure consistency).
- **Feet:** Speed (SPD is invaluable for taking more turns, stacking Streetwise Boxing faster, and generating Skill Points. ATK% is a fallback if you have exceptional SPD from substats).
- **Planar Sphere:** Physical DMG Boost (The single biggest multiplier for her damage).
- **Link Rope:** ATK% or Energy Regeneration Rate (ATK% is for pure damage. ERR can be considered if you find yourself desperately needing her AoE Ultimate up one turn earlier in tight rotations, but ATK% is generally superior for her role as a main DPS).

### Substats Priority
The order of importance is:
1. **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG:** Aim for a 1:2 ratio.
2. **ATK%:** Increases all her damage.
3. **Effect Hit Rate (EHR):** Needed to ensure her Bleed debuff lands consistently. A minimum of **30-40% EHR** is recommended before investing in more.
4. **Speed (SPD):** Helpful, but often less critical if you have the Speed main stat boots.
5. **Flat ATK:** Decent filler.

**Minimum Breakpoints:** Before worrying about substats, ensure you have the correct **Main Stats** on all relic pieces (CRIT/SPD/Phys%/ATK%). Then, aim for **~30% EHR** from substats to make her Bleed reliable. After that, stack as much CRIT and ATK% as possible.

## Stats Breakpoints & Targets

- **CRIT Rate:** Aim for **70%+**. This ensures consistent crits, especially with the Streetwise Boxing set. If using Inert Salsotto, you need less on substats.
- **CRIT DMG:** Aim for **140%+** after reaching your CRIT Rate target (a 1:2 ratio is ideal).
- **Speed:** **120+ SPD** is a good baseline. **134 SPD** is a key breakpoint for an extra turn in certain Memory of Chaos cycles.
- **Effect Hit Rate (EHR):** **30-40%** minimum to reliably apply Bleed. More is better if your team relies on it.
- **Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR):** Not critical, but having a Rope with ERR can help reach Ultimate thresholds one turn earlier.

**Why it works:** Asta provides a massive team-wide SPD and ATK buff, helping everyone act faster. March 7th offers shields and a chance to Freeze, providing defense. A second Physical character like Sushang helps break enemy Physical shields faster, increasing Bleed damage.

- **Break Focus:** Trailblazer, Silver Wolf (Weakness Implant), Asta, Gallagher

## Gameplay Tips & Rotation

**Skill Rotation:** The core rotation is straightforward: **Basic Attack → Skill → Basic Attack → Ultimate (when ready)**.
- Use her **Skill** on tough single targets or to hit two enemies at once for efficient SP usage.
- Use her **Basic Attack** on adjacent enemies or when saving SP for teammates.
- Her **Ultimate** is her main AoE nuke. Use it as soon as it's up during waves with multiple enemies to clear them quickly and stack Streetwise Boxing.

**Key Combos:**
- With **Bronya:** Trailblazer attacks → Bronya Skill → Trailblazer attacks again. This doubles her turn frequency.
- **Break Combo:** Use Trailblazer's Ultimate against Physical-weak enemies right after they take toughness damage to trigger massive Break DMG and apply a strong Bleed.

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
1. **Neglecting EHR:** Without it, her core Bleed mechanic won't land, costing you a lot of damage.
2. **Ignoring Speed:** Slow Trailblazer will fall behind in turns, reducing her damage output and stack-building.
3. **Using Ultimate on a Single Target:** Her Ultimate is AoE; save it for when you can hit 2+ enemies for maximum value.
4. **Forgetting she's Physical:** Always try to match her against Physical-weak enemies to maximize her Break potential.
