# Acheron Build Guide — Best Relics, Light Cones & Teams | GameBeliever

## Best Acheron Build Overview

**Role:** Hypercarry DPS | **Element:** Lightning | **Path:** Nihility  
The best **Acheron build** focuses on maximizing her devastating Ultimate, **Slashed Dream Cries in Red**, by stacking CRIT stats and leveraging her unique Defense Ignore mechanic. As a Nihility hypercarry, Acheron doesn't rely on Energy; instead, she gains stacks of Slashed Dream when allies inflict debuffs, making team synergy and her own high-damage output paramount. Acheron's kit is designed to obliterate entire enemy waves, especially when her Crimson Knot is triggered.

## Best Relics for Acheron

Choosing the **Acheron relics** that amplify her personal damage while synergizing with her debuff-reliant mechanic is key to unlocking her full potential.

### Primary Set (4pc): Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters
- **Why it's Best:** The 4-piece Pioneer set is the undisputed best-in-slot for **Acheron**. The 2-piece set provides a 12% DMG boost against debuffed enemies, which is virtually always active. The 4-piece set increases CRIT Rate by 12% against debuffed enemies and, more importantly, grants a stacking 24% CRIT DMG buff for each debuff the target suffers. Since Acheron's own kit and teams are built to apply debuffs, this set provides massive, unconditional stats.

### Alternative Sets (2pc + 2pc)
- **Passerby of Wandering Cloud (2pc) + Musketeer of Wild Wheat (2pc):** A simpler, more accessible combo. Passerby boosts Lightning DMG by 10%, and Musketeer boosts ATK by 12%. This is a solid early-game or transitional set before you farm a full Pioneer set.

### Planar Ornaments: Inert Salsotto
- **Why it's Best:** This ornament set directly boosts Acheron's two most important stats. The 2-piece set increases CRIT Rate by 8%, and when your current CRIT Rate is 50% or higher, it boosts Ultimate and follow-up attack DMG by 15%. This perfectly complements her playstyle, which revolves around massive Ultimate damage.
- **Alternative:** **Rutilant Arena** (2pc: Boosts Basic ATK and Skill DMG by 20% when CRIT Rate ≥ 70%) is a viable option, but less optimal since Acheron's core damage comes from her Ultimate, not her Basic or Skill.

## Best Light Cones for Acheron

Selecting the right **Acheron Light Cone** is critical to maximizing her damage ceiling. Her signature cone is transformative, but excellent alternatives exist.

### Best-in-Slot: **Along the Passing Shore** (Signature)
- **Why it's Best:** This is tailor-made for Acheron. It provides a massive 18% CRIT DMG boost. More importantly, it increases the wearer's DMG by 30% when they inflict debuffs, and it grants a stack of "Murky Abyss" (up to 2 stacks) for each debuff applied to an enemy, further boosting DMG by 15% per stack. This directly fuels her kit and provides unparalleled damage scaling.

### F2P / Alternative Options:
1. **Good Night Sleep Well:** An outstanding 4-star option. Each debuff on enemies increases the wearer's DMG by 12%, stacking up to 3 times (36% total DMG boost). Very easy to trigger in teams with consistent debuffers.
2. **Incessant Rain:** Another strong 4-star. Increases DMG dealt to debuffed enemies by 24%. It also reduces enemy DEF when the wearer uses their Basic ATK or Skill, which synergizes with her kit and team.
3. **We Will Kill Her (Nihility Herta Store Cone):** The best completely free-to-play option. It boosts DMG against enemies with lower DEF than the wearer, which aligns with her Defense Ignore mechanics.

## Recommended Stats & Substats

To build **Acheron** for maximum damage, focus on stats that amplify her Ultimate's devastation.

- **Body:** **CRIT Rate** or **CRIT DMG**. Aim for a 1:2 ratio (e.g., 80% CRIT Rate, 160% CRIT DMG). Her Major Traces and Light Cones often provide CRIT Rate, making a CRIT DMG body frequently optimal.
- **Feet:** **Speed (SPD)**. This is non-negotiable. Acheron needs to take turns to use her Skill, gain Slashed Dream points, and contribute to applying debuffs. Target **134 SPD** for the crucial extra turn in the first two cycles of Memory of Chaos.
- **Planar Sphere:** **Lightning DMG Boost**. This is her only source of elemental damage increase outside of substats and provides a raw multiplier to all her attacks.
- **Link Rope:** **ATK%**. Since she doesn't use Energy, ATK% is the direct damage multiplier she needs. Break Effect is not recommended.

### Substats Priority Order
1. **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG** (to reach your ratio target)
2. **Speed (SPD)** (to hit breakpoints)
3. **ATK%**
4. **Break Effect** (a nice bonus, but not a priority)

### Key Stat Thresholds
- **CRIT Rate:** Aim for 70%+ to fully activate the Inert Salsotto ornament bonus.
- **Speed:** **134 SPD** is the first major breakpoint. **120 SPD** is a minimum to avoid being too slow.

## Best Team Compositions

Acheron's team is built around applying as many debuffs as possible to rapidly charge her Slashed Dream stacks. Here are the top **Acheron team** setups.

### Team 1: The Debuff Engine (Premium)
- **Acheron / Silver Wolf / Pela / Sustain (Gallagher/Luocha)**
- **How it Works:** Silver Wolf and Pela are the ultimate debuff duo. Pela's Ultimate shreds enemy DEF, applying a debuff. Silver Wolf implants Weaknesses and applies multiple debuffs with her Skill and Ultimate. Gallagher can apply debuffs on his Ultimate and follow-up. This team maxes out Slashed Dream generation, allowing Acheron to Ult frequently.

### Team 2: F2P Debuff Core (Accessible)
- **Acheron / Pela / Guinaifen / Sustain (Natasha/Lynx)**
- **How it Works:** Pela remains the core debuffer. Guinaifen applies Burn with her Skill and Ultimate, and her talent further amplifies damage taken by enemies. This is a highly effective and more accessible team composition.

### Team 3: Break-Hybrid (Situational)
- **Acheron / Ruan Mei / Silver Wolf / Sustain**
- **How it Works:** Ruan Mei provides universal DMG boosts and All-Type RES PEN, which massively amplifies Acheron's already high damage. Silver Wolf handles debuff application. This team can overkill content but is incredibly powerful against bosses.

## Acheron Pull Recommendation

**Is Acheron worth pulling in the current meta?** Absolutely, but with considerations.

**Pull if:**
- You need a top-tier, meta-defining hypercarry to clear endgame content like Memory of Chaos and Pure Fiction.
- You enjoy powerful, flashy Ultimate-centric gameplay.
- You have or are willing to pull for her key teammates (Silver Wolf, Pela) and her signature Light Cone for maximum impact.
