# 丹恒·饮月 Build Guide — Best Relics, Light Cones & Teams | GameBeliever

## 丹恒·饮月 Build Overview

Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae is a premier 5-star Imaginary DPS following the Path of Destruction in Honkai: Star Rail. His core mechanic revolves around **Skill Point (SP) investment and consumption** to execute devastating enhanced Basic Attacks. Unlike traditional DPS units, his damage output is directly tied to the number of Skill Points he uses, making him a powerful but SP-hungry hypercarry who excels in teams built to generate ample points.

**Why this build works:** The optimal 丹恒·饮月 build prioritizes maximizing the damage of his Enhanced Basic Attack, which scales with his ATK and CRIT stats. By pairing him with the **4pc Imaginary set**, we leverage his inherent ability to apply Imprisonment, boosting his damage against slowed/Imprisoned enemies. His stats focus on CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, and ATK%, with a careful eye on SPD to ensure he acts in a beneficial order within his team. This approach transforms him into a relentless single-target and AoE powerhouse capable of clearing endgame content like Memory of Chaos.

**Stat Priorities:**
1.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG:** Aim for a 1:2 ratio as close as possible.
2.  **ATK%:** His main scaling stat.
3.  **Speed (SPD):** To control turn order and generate more SP over time.
4.  **Effect Hit Rate (EHR):** Only if you need to guarantee his Ultimate's Imprisonment land on high-resistance bosses.

## Best Relics for Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae

### Best 4pc Set: Wastelander of Banditry Desert (4pc)
This is his undisputed best-in-slot (BiS) 4-piece set. The 2-piece bonus provides a flat 10% Imaginary DMG boost, which is a straightforward and powerful increase. The 4-piece bonus is where it truly shines: it increases CRIT Rate by 10% against Imprisoned or slowed enemies. Since Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae's Ultimate applies Imprisonment, and his Imaginary break effect also inflicts Slow, he can consistently trigger this powerful CRIT Rate buff, making stat allocation much easier.

### Alternative Sets
- **2pc Wastelander of Banditry Desert + 2pc Musketeer of Wild Wheat:** This is a strong early-game or transitional set. The 2pc Imaginary DMG bonus from Wastelander pairs well with the universal 12% ATK boost from Musketeer. Use this until you farm a complete 4pc Wastelander set with good substats.
- **2pc Wastelander of Banditry Desert + 2pc Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters:** A niche but viable option if you struggle to maintain the 4pc Wastelander CRIT Rate buff but can consistently trigger the DoT/Persistent DMG effects of Pioneer (e.g., in teams with Kafka). The 12% DMG boost against debuffed enemies is potent.

### Best Planar Ornaments
- **Best-in-Slot: Inert Salsotto.** This is the definitive planar set for Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae. The 8% CRIT Rate boost helps meet his high CRIT needs, and the 15% Ultimate & Follow-up ATK DMG boost directly enhances his powerful Ultimate.
- **Alternative Options:**
    - **Rutilant Arena:** If you have exceptionally high CRIT Rate from substats (70%+), Rutilant's 20% Basic ATK & Skill DMG bonus becomes more valuable, especially since his Enhanced Basic Attack is his core damage source.
    - **Space Sealing Station:** A simpler, more accessible option providing a solid 24% ATK boost when SPD reaches 120. Good for early builds.

### Recommended Main Stats
- **Body:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (choose whichever you need more to approach the 1:2 ratio).
- **Feet:** **Speed (SPD)** is highly recommended. ATK% is a secondary option if you can reach 120+ SPD from substats and team buffs.
- **Planar Sphere:** **Imaginary DMG Boost.** This is a significant multiplier and is almost always the best choice.
- **Link Rope:** **ATK%** for maximum damage. **Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR)** can be considered to cycle his Ultimate faster for more Imprisonment uptime and burst damage, but ATK% is generally superior.

### Substats Priority
1.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG:** The top priority for all relic pieces.
2.  **ATK%:** A strong secondary stat.
3.  **Speed (SPD):** To reach key breakpoints.
4.  **Effect Hit Rate (EHR):** Only if your build lacks it and you want guaranteed Imprisonment on bosses (usually not needed).

**Minimum Breakpoints:** Aim for **70%+ CRIT Rate** (to activate Rutilant Arena's full effect if used) and as much CRIT DMG as possible. For Speed, **115-120 SPD** is a good target to ensure a smooth rotation.

## Stats Breakpoints & Targets

- **CRIT Rate Target:** **70% minimum** is ideal to activate Rutilant Arena fully and achieve a balanced ratio. With the 4pc Wastelander set, you can achieve this against debuffed enemies more easily.
- **CRIT DMG Target:** Aim for **140% or higher**. A 1:2 ratio with your CRIT Rate is the golden rule.
- **Speed (SPD) Breakpoints:**
    - **115 SPD:** A good baseline to ensure he acts a reasonable number of times.
    - **120 SPD:** Unlocks the full bonus from Space Sealing Station and allows for more SP generation over a battle.
    - **134 SPD+:** An aggressive breakpoint that allows for an extra turn in the first cycle of Memory of Chaos, highly recommended for advanced players.
- **ATK/EHR/ERR Targets:** Aim for **2500-3000+ ATK** (before battle buffs). EHR is usually not a priority (0-30% is sufficient). ERR is generally unnecessary unless using the ERR Rope for specific Ultimate-focused rotations.

## Gameplay Tips & Rotation

- **Core Rotation:** Your main goal is to generate 3 Skill Points to use on your Enhanced Basic Attack. A typical skill cycle is: Basic Attack (1 SP) → Basic Attack (1 SP) → Enhanced Basic Attack (3 SP). This sequence generates 2 SP for the team while unleashing your biggest hit.
- **Ultimate Usage:** Use your Ultimate when available, especially if it will apply Imprisonment to multiple key targets. The Imprisonment not only controls the enemy but also triggers your 4pc relic set's CRIT Rate bonus for follow-up attacks. Do not be afraid to use it for energy even on a single target if it helps your rotation.
- **Key Combo:** When paired with布洛妮娅, a powerful combo is: Dan Heng Enhanced Basic Attack → 布洛妮娅 Skill on Dan Heng → Dan Heng Enhanced Basic Attack. This allows for two powerful strikes in quick succession.
- **Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
    1.  **SP Mismanagement:** Never use his Enhanced Basic Attack unless you are certain you have enough SP for the team's upcoming turns. Forcing an SP-negative situation can cripple your supports and healers.
    2.  **Ignoring SPD:** Building Dan Heng too slow means fewer turns, less SP generation, and less damage output over time.
    3.  **Wasting Ultimate:** Using his Ultimate when only one low-priority enemy is left can be a waste. Aim to hit multiple elites or use it to reapply Imprisonment.
