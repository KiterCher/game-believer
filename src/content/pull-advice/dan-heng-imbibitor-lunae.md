# Should You Pull Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae? — Honkai Star Rail Pull Guide | GameBeliever

## Is Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae Worth Pulling?
**TL;DR: Worth Pulling (especially for meta-focused players and Imaginary coverage)**

Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae (DHIL) is a top-tier Imaginary DPS who offers unparalleled flexibility through his Enhanced Basic Attack mechanic. He excels in both single-target and AoE scenarios, making him exceptionally versatile for Memory of Chaos, Simulated Universe, and general content. **Pull him if** you lack a premium Imaginary damage dealer, value flexible SP consumption, or need a strong hypercarry. **Consider skipping if** you already have a well-invested Seele, lack key support units, or are saving for future Destruction characters.

**Who should pull this character:**
- Players needing a primary Imaginary DPS
- Those with strong SP-generating supports (Tingyun, Yukong, Pela)
- Account builders wanting a flexible, future-proof carry
- F2P players needing a powerful gacha DPS option

## Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae Value Analysis
### Current Meta Value: ★★★★★
DHIL remains one of the highest-damage characters in the game post-release. His unique Enhanced Basic Attack allows him to adjust his SP consumption and damage output turn-by-turn, a flexibility no other character possesses. He dominates Imaginary-weak content and performs excellently in Pure Fiction with the right team.

### Future-Proof Assessment: High
The Destruction path and Imaginary element are consistently valuable. His SP-flexible kit means he won't become obsolete when new SP-hungry supports release; instead, he'll adapt. Future supports that amplify Basic Attack damage or provide SP generation will directly buff him.

### Account Investment Priority:
- **New Accounts:** High Priority (build around him)
- **Established Accounts with Good DPS:** Medium (pull for coverage)
- **Accounts with Seele/Quantum Teams:** Low-Medium (optional upgrade)

### Comparison with Alternatives
| Character | Pros vs. DHIL | Cons vs. DHIL |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| **Seele** | More consistent single-target, free extra turns | Worse in AoE, relies on kill resets |
| **Blade** | Better skill-point efficiency, self-sustain | Lower burst damage, needs HP scaling |
| **Yanqing** | Strong single-target with shield | Extremely vulnerable without shield, no AoE |

**Verdict:** DHIL offers the best blend of raw damage, flexibility, and element coverage among current hypercarries.

## Best Team Synergies
### SP-Positive Teams (Enable Full Power DHIL)
1. **DHIL + Tingyun + Yukong + Luocha**  
   The premium "Dragon Boat" comp. Tingyun provides energy and ATK%, Yukong buffs Crit and Imaginary DMG, Luocha sustains without consuming SP. DHIL can use fully enhanced attacks every turn.

2. **DHIL + Asta + Pela + Fu Xuan**  
   Excellent F2P-friendly team. Asta's speed buff and Pela's DEF shred increase DHIL's damage significantly. Fu Xuan provides Crit Rate and mitigation.

### New Teams Enabled by DHIL
- **SP-Heavy Supports:** DHIL is one of the few DPS who can effectively use Yukong's ultimate and Bronya's skill without crippling the team's SP economy.
- **Basic Attack Buffers:** Future supports enhancing Basic Attack damage (like upcoming Ruan Mei) will directly increase his Enhanced Basic Attack output.

### Existing Teams He Improves
- **Mono Imaginary:** DHIL + Silver Wolf + Yukong + Luocha (guarantees Imaginary weakness implant)
- **Hypercarry Templates:** Replaces most existing hypercarries with better damage flexibility.

## Investment Requirements
### E0 Viable? **Absolutely.**
DHIL is complete at E0. His core mechanic—the Enhanced Basic Attack—is fully available. His Eidolons provide nice upgrades but aren't required for him to dominate content.

### Key Eidolons:
- **E1:** Nice but not essential (enhances follow-up attack)
- **E2:** Significant power spike (increases Enhanced Basic Attack multiplier)
- **E6:** Massive damage increase but overkill for all content
**Recommendation:** Stop at E0 or E2 if you have extra pulls.

### Light Cone Dependency?
- **Signature LC (Brighter Than the Sun):** ~15-20% damage increase. Provides ATK% and Crit Rate, easing build requirements.
- **Best F2P Alternative:** **S5 "Something Irreplaceable"** (Herta's shop) is excellent and only ~10% worse than signature.
- **Other Options:** **The Moles Welcome You** (Battle Pass) is also strong.

**Verdict:** Signature is great but not mandatory. He performs exceptionally well with free options.

### F2P Friendly?
**Yes, highly.** He works with 4-star supports (Tingyun, Yukong, Asta, Pela, March 7th) and has access to good F2P Light Cones. His damage output is high even without premium investment.

## FAQ

### 1. Is Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae good at E0?
**Yes, exceptionally good.** He's designed to work perfectly at E0. His Enhanced Basic Attack mechanic is available from the start, making him a complete character without any Eidolons.

### 2. Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae vs Seele: who to pull?
**Pull DHIL if:** You need AoE coverage, want flexible SP usage, or lack Imaginary DPS.  
**Pull Seele if:** You prefer faster gameplay, need pure single-target damage, or already have Imaginary coverage via Silver Wolf.  
**Account-wise:** DHIL is generally more versatile; Seele is faster for farming.

### 3. Does Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae need his signature Light Cone?
**No, he doesn't *need* it.** His signature ("Brighter Than the Sun") is his best-in-slot by a significant margin, but "Something Irreplaceable" (from Herta's shop) is an excellent F2P alternative that only lags behind by about 10-15% damage. He's still top-tier with free options.

### 4. What is the best build for Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae in HSR?
- **Relics:** 4pc Wastelander of Banditry Desert (imaginary set) or 2pc Wastelander + 2pc Musketeer
- **Planar Ornaments:** Inert Salsotto (ultimate/follow-up damage) or Rutilant Arena (basic attack damage)
- **Main Stats:** Crit Body, SPD or ATK% Boots, Imaginary DMG Sphere, ATK% Rope
- **Substats:** Crit Rate > Crit DMG > ATK% > SPD

### 5. What team is best for Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae?
The "Dragon Boat" premium team: **DHIL + Tingyun + Yukong + Luocha/Fu Xuan**. For F2P: **DHIL + Tingyun + Asta + Natasha/Gepard**. Silver Wolf is also excellent for implanting imaginary weakness.

### 6. What does Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae scale with in HSR?
He scales primarily with **ATK, Crit Rate, Crit DMG, and Imaginary DMG Bonus**. His Enhanced Basic Attacks have high multipliers that scale with these stats. Speed is less critical since his damage comes from enhanced basics, not turn frequency.

### 7. Is Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae a DPS or support?
**Pure DPS.** He's a hypercarry designed to deal damage. His kit has no support capabilities. He relies on supports to buff him and generate skill points.

### 8. What is Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae's best F2P light cone?
**"Something Irreplaceable"** (5-star from Herta's Light Cone Manifest shop) is his best F2P option. For 4-star options, **"The Moles Welcome You"** (Battle Pass) or **"Cruising in the Stellar Sea"** (Herta shop, if not using on Seele) work decently.

### 9. How to build Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae for maximum damage?
Prioritize **ATK% and Crit stats** on relics. Use the **Wastelander set** for Imaginary DMG. In battle, always use his **fully Enhanced Basic Attack (3 SP version)** when possible. Pair with **ATK buffers (Tingyun)** and **DEF shred (Pela)** for maximum damage.

### 10. Is Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae worth pulling in 2026?
**Yes, his value holds well.** His unique SP-flexible kit ensures he'll adapt to future team compositions. Imaginary remains a valuable element, and Destruction DPS are consistently strong. Unless a direct power creep emerges (unlikely soon), he'll remain meta-relevant.
