# 黑天鹅 Guide — Best Builds, Teams & More | GameBeliever

**Meta Description:** Master HSR Black Swan. This guide covers the best Black Swan build, optimal Light Cones, relics, and team comps to unleash her powerful DoT damage.

## Black Swan Overview: The Arcanist of DoT

**Black Swan** is a 5★ **Wind Nihility** character introduced in Version 2.0. She is not your typical damage-over-time (DoT) applicator; she is a **strategic debuffer and sub-DPS** who manipulates enemy vulnerabilities to explode with massive delayed damage. Her entire kit revolves around building and detonating unique stacks called **Arcana**, making her a cornerstone for dedicated DoT teams.

**Key Strengths:**
*   **Unrivaled DoT Amplifier:** Her signature "Epiphany" debuff makes enemies take increased DoT damage, functioning as a unique, powerful damage multiplier that no other character in HSR provides.
*   **Self-Sufficient Stack Application:** Black Swan applies and maintains Arcana stacks on enemies through multiple avenues—her Basic Attack, Skill, Ultimate, and even through allies' attacks with her Talent—ensuring consistent DoT pressure.
*   **High Area-of-Effect Potential:** Her Ultimate deals significant **Wind** damage to all enemies and guarantees all targets have Arcana stacks, making her exceptionally strong in multi-target scenarios like Memory of Chaos.

**Weaknesses:**
*   **Reliant on Teammates:** To truly maximize her damage, Black Swan needs allies who can also apply DoT effects to feed her Talent and trigger Arcana detonations more frequently.
*   **Slower Burst Potential:** Her damage is primarily delayed (DoT), which can be less efficient for quickly eliminating single, high-threat targets compared to traditional hypercarry DPS.
*   **Eidolon-Dependent Scaling:** While excellent at E0, her Eidolons (especially E1 and E2) significantly boost her debuffing capabilities and damage ceiling, making her performance more variable across investment levels.

**What Makes Her Special?**
While other Nihility characters like **Guinaifen** apply general DoTs or **Kafka** detonates them, **HSR Black Swan** occupies a unique niche: she is a **force multiplier for all DoT damage**. Her "Epiphany" state essentially turns her into a walking, talking damage buff for every DoT effect on the field. This synergy makes her irreplaceable in premium DoT compositions.

## Black Swan Skills Breakdown

Understanding how to layer Black Swan's abilities is key to playing her effectively.

*   **Basic Attack - Blasé:** Deals **Wind** DMG equal to a percentage of Black Swan's ATK to a single target. It has a fixed chance to apply one stack of **Arcana**. *Tactical Use:* Use this on Skill Points when your team is SP-starved. It’s your primary method for applying Arcana without consuming resources, and it counts as a DoT effect for her Talent.
*   **Skill - Decadence, False Twilight:** Deals **Wind** DMG to all enemies and has a high chance to apply two stacks of **Arcana**. Enemies already affected by Arcana have their existing stacks increased by one. *Tactical Use:* Your go-to for applying and maintaining Arcana on multiple enemies. The skill point cost is justified by its ability to build stacks quickly.
*   **Ultimate - Blumenkranz:** Cost: 100 Energy. Deals massive **Wind** DMG to all enemies, applies "Epiphany" for 2 turns, and guarantees all targets have at least one stack of Arcana. While under "Epiphany," enemies take **increased DoT DMG**, and all Arcana stacks are considered to be Wind Shear, Bleed, Burn, and Shock simultaneously for calculation purposes. *Tactical Use:* This is your ultimate combo starter. Use it when multiple enemies have high Arcana stacks to trigger a massive detonation. The universal DoT classification is its most powerful effect.
*   **Talent - From Self to Self:** When an ally attacks an enemy afflicted with Arcana, there is a fixed chance for Black Swan to deal additional **Wind** DoT DMG based on her ATK. The damage increases with the number of Arcana stacks on the target. *Tactical Use:* This incentivizes pairing Black Swan with fast-attacking allies or other DoT applicators (like Kafka). Every ally action becomes a potential Black Swan damage trigger.
*   **Technique - From Façade to Vérité:** Immediately enters a special state that increases her ATK for a short duration. Upon entering battle, it deals **Wind** DMG to all enemies and applies Arcana. *Tactical Use:* Excellent for pre-loading damage and starting fights with Arcana already applied, giving you an immediate advantage in Memory of Chaos or Simulated Universe.

**Major Traces & Eidolons:**
*   **Major Trace 1 (A2):** Increases the chance to apply Arcana stacks.
*   **Major Trace 2 (A4):** Black Swan's DoT effects are more likely to land.
*   **Major Trace 3 (A6):** When an enemy with Arcana is defeated, Black Swan gains a stack of "Epiphany," boosting her ATK.
*   **Eidolons:** Her E1 significantly boosts the "Epiphany" debuff effect. E2 makes her Talent's damage trigger more reliably and with higher base damage. These are massive power spikes.

## How Black Swan Fits in the Meta

**Current Meta Position:**
Black Swan is a **meta-defining character for the DoT archetype**. Following the release of Kafka and Ruan Mei, DoT teams have become one of the strongest and most consistent archetypes in Honkai Star Rail. Black Swan is the final, essential piece that elevates these teams from strong to elite. She is a top-tier unit for endgame content like **Pure Fiction** and **Memory of Chaos**, especially in nodes requiring sustained AoE damage.

**Best Content for Black Swan:**
*   **Memory of Chaos:** Excels in both single-target and multi-target floors due to her scaling mechanic.
*   **Pure Fiction:** Her Ultimate and Skill provide the AoE DoT application needed to clear waves of enemies.
*   **Simulated Universe (Path of Erudition/Nihility):** Her kit synergizes perfectly with Nihility blessings.

**Comparison with Similar Characters:**
*   **vs. Kafka:** They are teammates, not competitors. Kafka detonates DoTs immediately, while Black Swan builds them to a higher ceiling and amplifies their damage. You want both.
*   **vs. Guinaifen:** Guinaifen is an excellent budget option who applies Shock and a vulnerability debuff. Black Swan offers higher personal damage, a unique damage amplifier (Epiphany), and better scaling in the late game.

## FAQ

Here are answers to the most common questions about **Honkai Star Rail Black Swan**.

1.  **What is the best build for Black Swan in HSR?**
    The best **Black Swan build** focuses on an ATK% Body, SPD Boots, Wind DMG% Sphere, and ATK% Rope. Prioritize sub-stats in this order: Effect Hit Rate (to ~60-70%), Speed, ATK%. The relic set *Prisoner in Deep Confinement* is her signature best-in-slot.

2.  **What relics should I use for Black Swan?**
    Her top relic set is **Prisoner in Deep Confinement (4-piece)**, as it directly increases the damage of her DoTs. A strong alternative is **Eagle of Twilight Line (4-piece)** for more frequent Ultimates. For Planar Orbs, **Firmament Frontline: Glamoth** or **Pan-Cosmic Commercial Enterprise** are excellent choices.

3.  **What is Black Swan's best light cone?**
    Her signature Light Cone, **Reforged Remembrance**, is her undeniable best-in-slot. However, for alternatives, the 4★ **Eyes of the Prey** is a fantastic option that provides both Effect Hit Rate and DoT DMG boost. The Battle Pass light cone **Incessant Rain** is also very strong.

4.  **What team is best for Black Swan?**
    The best **Black Swan team** is the premium DoT composition: Black Swan, Kafka, Ruan Mei, and a healer like Huohuo. This team maximizes her unique mechanics through constant DoT application, detonation, and damage amplification.

5.  **Is Black Swan good in Honkai Star Rail?**
    Yes, she is exceptional. As a core member of the powerful DoT meta, Black Swan is a top-tier unit for clearing the game's hardest content. She is highly recommended for players who enjoy strategic, damage-over-time gameplay.

6.  **What does Black Swan scale with in HSR?**
    Black Swan's damage and debuff effects scale primarily with **ATK%**. Additionally, her ability to land Arcana and the Epiphany debuff is heavily dependent on her **Effect Hit Rate** stat. This makes a hybrid ATK/EHR build necessary.

7.  **Is Black Swan a DPS or support?**
    She is a hybrid **Debuffer/Sub-DPS**. While she applies powerful debuffs like Epiphany that support the team, her personal damage contribution—especially from her Talent and Ultimate—is substantial, often making her one of the top damage dealers in her teams.

8.  **What is Black Swan's best F2P light cone?**
    Among non-limited options, **Eyes of the Prey** (4★) is her best F2P-friendly light cone. If you don't have that, **Tutorial Mission** from the Herta Store is a solid budget choice that helps with Energy regeneration and Effect Hit Rate.
