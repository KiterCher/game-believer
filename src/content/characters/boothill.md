## Boothill Overview: The Galactic Bounty Hunter

Boothill is a 5★ Physical character following the Hunt path, released in Honkai: Star Rail version 2.2. As a member of the Galaxy Rangers, he is the game's premier **Boothill Physical** single-target burst DPS, specializing in breaking enemy toughness bars and capitalizing on Break effects. His gameplay revolves around setting up a tactical "Standoff" to unleash devastating, focused damage.

**Key Strengths:**
1.  **Unmatched Break Effect Scaling:** Unlike other Hunt characters who focus purely on Crit, a significant portion of Boothill's damage comes from Break Effect. His Talent and Eidolons further amplify Break DMG and Bleed application, making him a unique "Break DPS."
2.  **Self-Sufficient Burst:** His Ultimate grants him "Pocket Trickshot" charges, allowing for powerful follow-up attacks without relying on ally turns. This provides incredible action economy and burst potential.
3.  **Tactical "Standoff" Mechanic:** His Skill places him in a Standoff, reducing damage taken but increasing damage dealt. This creates a high-risk, high-reward gameplay style that rewards careful positioning and turn management.

**Weaknesses:**
1.  **Slow Base Speed:** With a base SPD of 100, Boothill is one of the slower characters, making him reliant on external SPD buffs or specific Light Cones to act frequently.
2.  **Vulnerable in Standoff:** While in Standoff, he becomes the primary target for enemies. Without proper sustain or shielding, this can lead to quick defeats.
3.  **Single-Target Focus:** While excellent for bosses and elite enemies, his kit lacks AoE capabilities, making him less versatile in waves of small mobs compared to Destruction or Erudition characters.

**What Makes Him Special?**
Boothill is not your typical Crit-based Hunt DPS. He is a **Boothill Physical Break** specialist. His value lies in his ability to shred toughness bars and deal massive damage that scales with Break Effect, a stat most other DPS characters ignore. This makes him a unique and powerful option in a meta that increasingly values Break mechanics and weakness exploitation.

## Boothill Skills Breakdown: A Marksman's Toolkit

**Basic Attack: Single Out**
Deals Physical DMG equal to 50% of Boothill's ATK to a single target enemy.
*Tactical Use:* Your primary attack for generating Skill Points and building Ultimate energy when not using your Skill.

**Skill: Pocket Trickshot (Standoff)**
Enters the Standoff state. In this state, all other allies are disabled, and Boothill becomes the sole target of enemy attacks. He gains increased DMG taken but deals significantly increased DMG. Ends after 3 of his turns or when defeated.
*Tactical Use:* This is the core of Boothill's kit. Activate this before unleashing your Ultimate and follow-up attacks to maximize burst damage. Plan your team's survivability around this vulnerable period.

**Ultimate: Tumbleweed**
Deals Physical DMG equal to 360% of Boothill's ATK to a single enemy. If the target is in Weakness Break, the DMG is further increased. Grants 1 "Pocket Trickshot" charge.
*Tactical Use:* Your main nuke. Always aim to use this on an enemy whose toughness bar is broken for massive bonus damage. The charge gained fuels your Talent's follow-up attack.

**Talent: Three-Shot Savant**
When Boothill's Pocket Trickshot charge is consumed or he breaks an enemy's Weakness, he deals additional Physical DMG equal to 100% of his Break Effect to the target. This scales immensely with his **Boothill Best Build**.
*Tactical Use:* This passive is the source of his Break Damage. Focus on breaking enemy toughness bars to trigger this powerful bonus hit repeatedly.

**Technique: Bounty**
Increases the DMG dealt by the next battle by 30%.
*Tactical Use:* Use before challenging elite enemies or bosses for a straightforward damage boost at the start of the encounter.

## How Boothill Fits in the Meta: The Break Specialist

In the current Honkai: Star Rail meta, **HSR Boothill** carves out a unique niche. He is not the fastest single-target dealer like Seele, nor the most versatile like Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae. However, he is arguably the best character at exploiting Physical Weakness and generating Break DMG.

**Best Teams for Boothill:** He excels in teams built around him as the sole hyper-carry, supported by characters who can accelerate his actions, protect him during Standoff, and amplify his Break Effect. Popular synergies include **Harmony** characters like Ruan Mei and Asta, and **Preservation/Abundance** units like Fu Xuan or Luocha to keep him alive.

**Best Content:** Boothill is S-tier for single-target boss fights and Memory of Chaos stages with Physical-weak elites. His ability to quickly break shields and deal enormous follow-up damage makes him a premier choice for end-game content focused on defeating powerful, single entities.

**Comparison with Similar Characters:**
*   **vs. Seele (Hunt, Quantum):** Seele offers more self-action and turn resets, making her faster and better for clearing multiple weaker enemies. Boothill deals higher upfront burst and Break Damage on single targets.
*   **vs. Sushang (Hunt, Physical):** Sushang is a 4★ version with similar Break focus but less raw power and a simpler kit. Boothill is a significant upgrade in damage potential and utility for dedicated Break teams.

## FAQ: Answering Your Boothill Questions

**1. What is the best build for Boothill in HSR?**
The **Boothill Best Build** focuses on Break Effect% on his Body relic, ATK% or SPD on his Boots, Physical DMG% on his Sphere, and a Break Effect or ATK% Rope. Prioritize Crit Rate/Damage and SPD substats.

**2. What relics should I use for Boothill?**
The best **Boothill Relics** are the 4-piece "Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge" set for its Break Effect bonuses, or a 2-piece mix of "Genius of Brilliant Stars" (Physical DMG) and "Talia: Kingdom of Banditry" (Break Effect & SPD). The planar set "Rutilant Arena" (Crit Rate & Skill DMG) is also excellent.

**3. What is Boothill's best light cone?**
His signature 5-star Light Cone, "Sailing Towards a Second Life," is his absolute best in slot. Excellent 4-star alternatives include "Swordplay" (for DMG stacking) and "Only Silence Remains" (for ATK% and Crit DMG). See our full **[Boothill Light Cone Tier List](/lightcones/boothill-tierlist)**.

**4. What team is best for Boothill?**
The **Boothill Best Team** typically includes a Break Efficiency buffer like Ruan Mei, a SPD buffer like Asta or Bronya, and a reliable sustain. The "Break Hyper-Carry" team mentioned in this guide is the top meta choice.

**5. Is Boothill good in Honkai Star Rail?**
Yes, **Honkai Star Rail Boothill** is a top-tier single-target Physical DPS. He is particularly strong in the current meta for challenging endgame content like Memory of Chaos due to his exceptional Weakness Break capabilities.

**6. What does Boothill scale with in HSR?**
Boothill scales uniquely with two main stats: **Break Effect** (for his Talent's bonus damage) and the traditional DPS stats of **ATK%**, **Crit Rate**, and **Crit DMG** (for his direct attacks and Ultimate).

**7. Is Boothill a DPS or support?**
Boothill is unequivocally a **DPS** (Damage Per Second) character. His entire kit is designed to deal damage, not to support allies.

**8. Is Boothill worth pulling in 2026?**
If your account lacks a strong single-target Physical DPS or you enjoy Break-focused gameplay, **Boothill** remains a powerful and unique character to own. His meta value is high for specific endgame challenges.
