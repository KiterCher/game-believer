## Gallagher Overview

Gallagher is a unique 4-star Fire character who joins the roster as an **Abundance Path** healer, first introduced in Version 2.1 during the Penacony saga. While his rarity is four stars, don't let that fool you—**Honkai Star Rail Gallagher** has carved out a significant niche in the meta. His primary role is as a **healer and sustain**, but what makes him truly special is his powerful offensive utility and unique scaling mechanics. He isn't just a passive healer; he actively contributes to the team's damage output and Break efficiency.

**Key Strengths:**
*   **Off-Turn Healing & Cleanse:** Gallagher provides consistent healing without consuming a turn via his Skill's Besotted state, making him exceptionally skill-point efficient. His Ultimate also offers a team-wide cleanse, a highly valuable utility.
*   **Break Effect Scaling:** Unlike most traditional healers who scale purely with HP, Gallagher's healing and personal damage scale with **Break Effect**, opening up unique build paths.
*   **Fire Break & Burn Application:** His kit naturally applies Fire weakness and Burn, making him a fantastic partner for Break-focused teams and increasing overall DoT (Damage over Time) pressure.

**Key Weaknesses:**
*   **Conditional Healing:** His powerful healing requires careful management of the Besotted state and his Ultimate. Mistiming can leave your team vulnerable.
*   **Lower Base Healing:** Compared to premium 5-star Abundance units, his raw healing numbers are lower, requiring more investment in stats.
*   **Eidolon Dependent:** While functional at E0, key Eidolons (especially E2 and E6) dramatically increase his utility, healing output, and damage potential.

**What Makes Gallagher Special?**
Compared to other healers like **Luocha** or **Natasha**, Gallagher isn't about providing raw, unconditional healing. He is a **proactive support healer**. He thrives in aggressive, Break-oriented teams where he can contribute to Breaking enemies, applying debuffs, and dishing out surprisingly high damage—all while keeping the team alive. He is the poster child for the "healer who does more than just heal" archetype.

## Gallagher Skills Breakdown

Understanding his kit is crucial to mastering the **Gallagher Guide**.

*   **Basic Attack - Dazzling Dagger:** A standard single-target Fire DMG attack. Its primary use is to generate Skill Points and deal minor damage.
*   **Skill - Nectar Blitz:** This is the core of his kit. He consumes his own HP to deal Fire DMG and applies **Besotted** to a single ally. **Besotted** is a unique state that heals the target each time they take action and increases their Break DMG. This is his primary, efficient healing tool.
*   **Ultimate -Champagne Etiquette:** A powerful AoE Fire DMG attack that also applies the **Besotted** state to ALL allies. It also inflicts a unique debuff, **Besotted**, on enemies, increasing the Break DMG they receive. This is his best skill for team-wide sustain and offense.
*   **Talent - Particular Drama:** Passive talent that increases his Effect RES and grants a healing bonus based on his Break Effect. This directly incentivizes building Break Effect on him.
*   **Technique - On the House:** Enters battle and immediately inflicts Besotted on all enemies, increasing the Break DMG they take for the first few turns. A fantastic opener for aggressive teams.

**Unique Mechanic:** The **Besotted** state he applies is the key to his identity. It provides healing over time and amplifies Break Damage, perfectly synergizing with his Fire element and Break Effect scaling.

The current **Honkai Star Rail Gallagher** meta position is highly favorable, especially with the rise of Break-centric team compositions. He is one of the best 4-star sustain options in the game.

**Best Teams:** Gallagher shines in teams built around **Break Efficiency** and **Fire Weakness**. He is a perfect fit for:
*   **Super Break Teams:** Pairing him with characters like **Firefly** or **Ruan Mei** creates a devastating cycle of breaking and regenerating energy.

## FAQ

**1. What is the best build for Gallagher in HSR?**
The **Gallagher Best Build** prioritizes **Break Effect** (aim for 150%+), followed by **Speed** (134+) and HP%. Use the **Talia: Kingdom of Banditry** relic set and an **Effect Hit Rate** body piece if struggling to land debuffs. His playstyle revolves around using his Skill on your main DPS and timing his Ultimate for team-wide sustain and Break amplification.

**2. What relics should I use for Gallagher?**
For his **Gallagher Relics**, the optimal 4-piece set is **Talia: Kingdom of Banditry** for the massive Break Effect boost. For 2-piece sets, pair **Thief of Shooting Meteor** with **Messenger Traversing Hackerspace** for Speed and Break Effect. The 2-piece **Watchmaker, Master of Dream Machinist** is also excellent for its Break Effect bonus. For Planar Orbons, **Fleet of the Ageless** or **Penacony, Land of the Dreams** are strong choices.

**3. What is Gallagher's best light cone?**
Gallagher's best-in-slot 4-star Light Cone is unequivocally his signature weapon, **What Is Real?**. It provides high base HP, Break Effect, and a healing boost that synergizes perfectly. For other **Gallagher Light Cone** options, 5-star cones like **Time Waits for No One** are good, but his signature 4-star is often more effective for his specific build.

**4. What team is best for Gallagher?**
His absolute best team is a **Super Break composition** with **Firefly**, **Ruan Mei**, and **Harmony Trailblazer**. This team maximizes his Fire Break application and Break DMG amplification. For a more general **Gallagher Team**, any composition that benefits from consistent, efficient healing and Fire application will work well.

**5. Is Gallagher good in Honkai Star Rail?**
Yes, absolutely. The **HSR Gallagher** is considered one of the best 4-star characters and a top-tier healer. He excels in endgame content like Memory of Chaos and Pure Fiction, especially in Break-focused metas. His kit offers high value and utility that rivals many 5-star alternatives.

**6. What does Gallagher scale with in HSR?**
Gallagher uniquely scales with **Break Effect** for both his healing (via his Talent) and his personal damage. He also scales with **Speed** to act more frequently, **HP%** for survivability, and **Effect Hit Rate** to ensure his debuffs land. This is different from most healers who scale primarily with HP% and Outgoing Healing Boost.

**7. Is Gallagher a DPS or support?**
Gallagher is a **hybrid Support**. His primary role is **healing and sustain** (Support), but a significant portion of his kit is dedicated to dealing **Fire Break Damage and applying debuffs** (sub-DPS). This dual nature is what makes him so versatile and powerful in the current meta.

**8. What is Gallagher's best F2P light cone?**
For free-to-play players, the best 3-star Light Cone for Gallagher is **Arrows** for the extra Break Effect, or **Loop** for a small healing boost. However, crafting his signature 4-star **What Is Real?** from Herta's Shop or obtaining it from banners is the recommended long-term goal for any **Gallagher Build**.

**9. How to build Gallagher for maximum damage?**
To build Gallagher for maximum **personal damage**, stack **Break Effect** as high as possible (200%+), use an **ATK%** or **Fire DMG Boost** Planar Sphere, and pair him with buffers like Ruan Mei who boost Break Damage. However, note that building purely for damage can compromise his survivability and healing. A balanced build is often more practical.

**10. Is Gallagher worth pulling in 2026?**
Considering his release in 2.1, Gallagher will remain highly relevant as long as Break teams are meta. Given his exceptional kit for a 4-star, he is absolutely worth using and building if you acquire him. His value doesn't diminish with time due to his unique and powerful mechanics.
