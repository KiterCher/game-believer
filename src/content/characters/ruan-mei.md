## Ruan Mei Overview

Ruan Mei is a 5★ Ice character following the **Ruan Mei Harmony** path, a member of the Divination Commission introduced in Version 1.6. She isn't just another generic buffer; Ruan Mei HSR has carved out a unique niche as the premier enabler for Break-effect-focused teams. While other Harmony units like Bronya or Sparkle excel at advancing turns and amplifying Crit DPS, Ruan Mei's kit revolves around a fundamental game mechanic: Weakness Break. Her core strength lies in providing team-wide Speed, massive Break Efficiency buffs, and, after her Ultimate, a powerful resistance-shred field that debuffs enemies.

**Key Strengths:**
1.  **Unparalleled Break Support:** Her signature capability. Ruan Mei drastically increases the rate at which allies deplete enemy Toughness bars, accelerating Weakness Breaks for increased damage and crowd control.
2.  **Team-Wide Utility:** Provides consistent Speed boosts to the entire party via her Skill and significant DMG increases to broken or delayed enemies with her Ultimate.
3.  **Enhanced Freeze Utility:** As an **Honkai Star Rail Ruan Mei** (HSR Ruan Mei), she has a unique interaction with Freeze. Her Ultimate extends the delay of enemies already Weakness Broken, and her Talent has a chance to apply Freeze when an enemy's weakness is broken.

**Weaknesses:**
1.  **Niche Dependency:** Her value peaks in teams that rely on frequent Weakness Breaks. In hypercarry Crit teams that ignore Break mechanics, her contribution is less pronounced than other top-tier buffers.
2.  **Eidolon & Light Cone Reliance:** While functional at E0 with F2P gear, her signature Light Cone and Eidolon upgrades (especially E1 and E2) significantly amplify her supportive power.
3.  **No Direct Action Advancement:** Unlike Bronya or Sparkle, she doesn't provide a "second turn," which can be a drawback for certain DPS playstyles.

What makes **Ruan Mei Guide** content stand out is her role as a meta-defining pillar for Break teams, a playstyle that has surged in prominence. She doesn't just buff raw stats; she fundamentally alters how your team interacts with enemy Toughness.

## Ruan Mei Skills Breakdown

Understanding **Ruan Mei's** kit is key to maximizing her impact.

*   **Basic Attack - Thorny Caltrops:** Deals Ice DMG to a single enemy. Simple, but applies a special debuff (Caltrop) that lasts until the enemy's next action, interacting with some of her other abilities.
*   **Skill - Weave: Incessant Rain:** This is the core of her team support. After using this Skill, Ruan Mei grants all allies a 10% Speed increase and a 50% Weakness Break Efficiency boost for 3 turns. **Tactical Use:** Use this Skill at the start of combat and maintain it. The Break Efficiency buff is what makes her unique, allowing even slow or single-hit characters to break enemies rapidly.
*   **Ultimate - Petals to Stream, Repose in Dream:** Ruan Mei deploys a field for 2 turns that increases all allies' All-type DMG Bonus by 25%. When an enemy in this field is Weakness Broken, they receive additional Ice DMG and their Delay is extended by 50%. **Tactical Use:** Time this Ultimate to coincide with your team's burst window. Pop it right before your DPS characters are about to break multiple enemies for devastating follow-up damage and extended crowd control.
*   **Talent - Solitude of Thinking:** At the start of battle, Ruan Mei increases all allies' Break Effect by 12% (scales with her Break Effect). Additionally, when an enemy is Weakness Broken by any ally, there is a 40% base chance it will also be inflicted with Freeze. **Tactical Use:** This passive further synergizes with Break-focused teams, adding a layer of CC and supplementary damage.
*   **Technique - The Plum Blossom Fortunes:** Using this technique before battle increases the allies' Break Effect for the first 2 turns of combat. **Tactical Use:** Always use this before challenging Memory of Chaos, Simulated Universe, or difficult content to give your team an early advantage.

## How Ruan Mei Fits in the Meta

**Ruan Mei Best Team** compositions define the current meta for Break-centric playstyles. She is the engine that powers teams featuring DPS characters who benefit immensely from breaking enemy weaknesses, such as **Boothill**, **Firefly**, and **Xueyi**. She is also a fantastic, albeit less optimal, support for Damage over Time (DoT) teams, as the Break Efficiency helps trigger more DoT detonations via Weakness Break.

**Content She Excels In:**
*   **Memory of Chaos & Pure Fiction:** Her team-wide buffs and Break control are invaluable for clearing waves and managing tough elites.
*   **Simulated Universe (Divergent Universe):** Break-oriented blessings synergize perfectly with her kit.
*   **Apocalyptic Shadow:** The endgame mode focused on Weakness Break is practically tailored for her.

**Comparison with Similar Characters:**
*   **vs. Bronya:** Bronya is superior for advancing a single Crit DPS's turns for raw damage output. Ruan Mei is superior for team-wide consistency and enabling Break teams.
*   **vs. Sparkle:** Similar to Bronya, Sparkle excels in Skill Point generation and Crit Damage boosting for specific DPS. Ruan Mei offers a more universal, meta-agnostic utility based on game mechanics.
*   **vs. Pela:** Both shred defenses, but Pela is single-target/aoe with her Ultimate, while Ruan Mei's is a persistent field. Ruan Mei's added Speed and Break Efficiency give her broader team utility beyond just DEF shred.

## FAQ

Here are answers to the most common questions about building and using **HSR Ruan Mei**.

**1. What is the best build for Ruan Mei in HSR?**
The **Ruan Mei Best Build** focuses on a Break Effect/Speed hybrid. Aim for 180%+ Break Effect (to max her Talent), 145+ Speed (with her Skill), and high Energy Regeneration Rate. Her signature Light Cone, "Past Self in Mirror," is best-in-slot, but "Meshing Cogs" or "Memories of the Past" are excellent F2P options.

**2. What relics should I use for Ruan Mei?**
The two-piece set **Talia: Kingdom of Banditry** (for Break Effect and Speed) is core. For the four-piece set, **Messenger Traversing Hackerspace** (for team Speed) or **Guard of Wuthering Snow** (for survivability) are top choices. A hybrid 2pc/2pc Break set can also work. Our **Ruan Mei Relics** guide has detailed stat priorities.

**3. What is Ruan Mei's best light cone?**
Her signature 5-star Light Cone, "Past Self in Mirror," is undeniably her **Ruan Mei Best Light Cone**, providing Break Effect, Skill Point recovery, and an Ultimate DMG boost for the team. Strong 4-star alternatives include "Meshing Cogs" (for Energy) and "Memories of the Past" (for Break Effect).

**4. What team is best for Ruan Mei?**
The **Ruan Mei Best Team** is a Break-focused composition. Pair her with DPS characters who inflict Weakness Break easily, like Firefly, Boothill, or Xueyi. She also pairs well with DoT characters like Kafka. Avoid pairing her solely with Crit DPS who have no Break mechanic synergy.

**5. Is Ruan Mei good in Honkai Star Rail?**
Yes, she is exceptionally good and meta-defining. **Ruan Mei Guide** analyses consistently place her as a top-tier support, especially for endgame content that emphasizes Weakness Break. Her unique utility makes her a valuable pull.

**6. What does Ruan Mei scale with in HSR?**
Her primary scaling stat is **Break Effect**, which directly powers her Talent. She also benefits immensely from **Speed** (to apply buffs faster) and **Energy Regeneration Rate** (to maintain Ultimate uptime).

**7. Is Ruan Mei a DPS or support?**
She is a pure Support character. While her skills deal some Ice DMG, her kit's value comes entirely from buffing her team's Speed, Break Efficiency, DMG, and applying debuffs to enemies. Think of her as a "Break Enabler."

**8. How to build Ruan Mei for maximum damage?**
While she is a support, you can build her for maximum *break-related* damage. Stack Break Effect as high as possible and ensure her Ultimate is always active to exploit the resistance shred and extended delays against broken enemies. This "damage" comes from enabling your team and the supplementary damage from breaks.
