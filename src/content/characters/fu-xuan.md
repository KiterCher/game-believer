## Fu Xuan Overview: The Quantum Matrix of Sustain

**Rarity:** 5★  
**Element:** Quantum  
**Path:** Preservation  
**Faction:** Divination Commission  
**Release Version:** 1.3  
**Role:** Defensive Support / Damage Mitigation Tank

In the ever-shifting meta of Honkai: Star Rail, **Fu Xuan** has cemented her status as one of the most uniquely powerful Preservation characters available. Unlike traditional shielders or taunt-based tanks, Fu Xuan operates on a revolutionary principle of **damage distribution**. She doesn't just protect your team; she fundamentally alters the rules of engagement by taking a portion of all damage intended for allies onto herself.

**Key Strengths:**
1.  **Unmatched Teamwide Damage Mitigation:** Her signature ability to intercept and redistribute damage makes her arguably the best pure damage reduction unit in the game, significantly lowering the healing burden on your sustain.
2.  **Permanent HP & CRIT Buffs:** Her unique Matrix of Prescience grants a massive, permanent Max HP increase to all allies. Her Ultimate and certain Eidolons further boost CRIT Rate, providing universal offensive benefits rarely seen on a tank.
3.  **Self-Sustain & Durability:** Fu Xuan is built to take a beating. Her HP scaling, self-healing from her Skill and Talent, and built-in damage reduction make her exceptionally durable, allowing her to function as both the team's damage sponge and primary sustainer in many scenarios.

**Weaknesses:**
1.  **Vulnerable to One-Shots:** If a single enemy attack is powerful enough to exceed her remaining HP and damage reduction, she can be knocked out instantly, potentially causing a team wipe. Managing her HP is crucial.
2.  **Less Effective Against Massive AoE Spam:** While she excels against focused damage, encounters with relentless, high-damage Area of Effect (AoE) attacks can strain her HP pool more than a shielder who negates the damage entirely.
3.  **Light Cone & Relic Dependent:** To reach her maximum potential, especially in higher-difficulty content like Memory of Chaos (MoC), Fu Xuan requires specific gear to maximize her HP, DEF, and Energy Regeneration.

**What Makes Her Special?**
**Honkai Star Rail Fu Xuan** isn't just another tank; she's a *meta-defining support*. She provides the three pillars of team survival—damage mitigation, HP buffing, and CRIT buffs—in a single package. This allows DPS characters to build more aggressively and enables the use of support characters who offer fewer defensive utilities but greater offensive amplification, like Sparkle or Bronya. Compared to a shielder like **Gepard**, who prevents damage but can have downtime between shields, Fu Xuan offers constant, unconditional mitigation. Compared to the **Fire Trailblazer**, who focuses on taunting and providing small shields, Fu Xuan's damage redistribution is more universally effective against both single-target and AoE attacks.

## Fu Xuan Skills Breakdown: The Matrix of Prescience

**Basic Attack: Thoughtful Premonition**  
Deals Quantum DMG equal to 50% of Fu Xuan's Max HP to a single target enemy. Her ATK scaling is intentionally low; this is primarily a tool to generate Skill Points.

**Skill: Known by Stars, Shown by Hearts**  
This is the core of her kit. Fu Xuan enters the **Matrix of Prescience** state for 3 turns. While active:
*   She gains the **"Prana-Siphoned"** effect, which is the key to her damage redistribution.
*   All allies (including herself) have their **Max HP increased by a significant percentage of Fu Xuan's Max HP**.
*   Fu Xuan takes on **65% of the damage** that allies would receive. This damage is mitigated based on her **DEF and Damage Reduction** stats before being subtracted from her HP.
*   Using her Skill again refreshes its duration. Managing this state is vital for continuous team protection.

**Ultimate: Woes of Many Morphed Into One**  
Fu Xuan deals Quantum DMG equal to 100% of her Max HP to all enemies and restores her own HP by an amount equal to 50% of the damage dealt. This is her primary self-healing tool and provides solid AoE damage, especially given her HP scaling. **Energy Cost: 100.**

**Talent: Calamity Repellence**  
When Fu Xuan's HP drops below 50%, she immediately gains a **HP Recovery** equal to 20% of her Max HP. This can trigger once every 6 turns. This passive is a critical safety net that enhances her solo sustain capability.

**Technique: Omniscience in an Instant**  
After using Technique, Fu Xuan creates a dimension that lasts for 15 seconds. Enemies within this dimension are affected by **"All-Seeing"**, which increases the DMG they take. This is an excellent pre-battle buff, especially for Simulated Universe (SU) and Memory of Chaos.

## How Fu Xuan Fits in the Meta: A Preservation Powerhouse

**Current Meta Position:**  
**HSR Fu Xuan** is firmly in the **S+ tier** for defensive supports. She is considered the best all-around Preservation character for comfortable and efficient clears in all high-level content, particularly **Memory of Chaos (MoC) and pure fiction**. Her presence simplifies survival, allowing players to focus on offensive output.

**Best Teams for This Character:**  
Fu Xuan excels in teams that want to minimize time spent on survival and maximize offensive momentum. She is the premier partner for "Hypercarry" teams.
*   **Premium Hypercarry (Fu Xuan + Sparkle + Silver Wolf + DPS):** This is the pinnacle **Fu Xuan Best Team** composition. Sparkle provides massive CRIT DMG and Skill Points, Silver Wolf implants weaknesses and shreds DEF, and the DPS (like Seele, Qingque, or even a well-built Luocha) destroys the enemy. Fu Xuan's CRIT Rate buff stacks beautifully with Sparkle's CRIT DMG buff.
*   **Sustain-DPS Hybrid (Fu Xuan + Blade + Ruan Mei + Huohuo):** Fu Xuan's HP buff directly increases Blade's damage output. With Ruan Mei boosting Break Effect and DMG, and Huohuo providing energy and ATK buffs, this team can both deal immense damage and survive indefinitely, as Blade's HP drain and Fu Xuan's mitigation complement each other perfectly.
*   **F2P-Friendly Core (Fu Xuan + Asta/MC (Harmony) + Any DPS):** Fu Xuan's kit is so powerful that she can carry the defensive load of a team even with less optimal supports. Asta's Speed buff allows your DPS to act more often, while Fu Xuan keeps everyone alive and buffed.

**Content Suitability:**  
She is universally excellent but especially dominant in:
*   **Memory of Chaos:** Reduces the need for a second survival character.
*   **Simulated Universe (Divergent Universe):** Her survivability and the **"Path Resonance: Preservation"** work together perfectly.
*   **Boss Rushes:** Her constant mitigation prevents chip damage from wearing down the team.

## Best Build for Fu Xuan: Balancing HP and Mitigation

The ultimate **Fu Xuan Build** revolves around three core stats: **Max HP, DEF, and Energy Regeneration Rate**. Her damage and the strength of her HP buff scale with HP, while her survival depends on HP and DEF. Energy Regeneration ensures her Ultimate (and thus her self-heal) is available frequently.

*   **Link to Full Build:** For a detailed breakdown of relic main stats, substat priorities, and all options, visit our **Fu Xuan General Build Guide**.
*   **Why This Build Works:** A high HP pool increases both her damage and the team-wide HP buff. Sufficient DEF ensures the damage she takes is manageable. **Energy Regeneration** (preferably via a Rope and/or Light Cone) is non-negotiable for maintaining her Ultimate uptime, which is critical for her long-term survival.

**Fu Xuan Relics:**
*   **Best in Slot (BiS):** **2-Piece Passerby of Wandering Cloud** (HP +10%) / **2-Piece Guard of Wuthering Snow** (DMG Reduction when HP <50%) or **2-Piece Belobog of the Architects** (DEF +15%). This combination directly boosts her survivability and mitigation.
*   **Alternative:** **4-Piece Longevous Disciple**. If you're building her with a DPS who scales with HP loss (like Blade), this set can be a niche but powerful option, granting her stacks as she takes damage from redistribution.
*   **Planar Ornaments:** **Fleet of the Ageless** (HP +12%, team ATK buff) is the universal best option. **Penacony, Land of the Dreams** can be considered for her in a Mono-Quantum team.

**Fu Xuan Light Cone:**
*   **BiS:** **She Already Shut Her Eyes** (Signature Light Cone). It provides massive HP, DEF, and an unconditional team DMG Reduction effect, perfectly synergizing with her kit.
*   **Best 4-Star Option:** **Moment of Victory** (from the Herta Store). Increases DEF and aggro, with a DEF boost when attacked. Excellent for building her stats.
*   **Best F2P Light Cone:** **Trend of the Universal Market**. The burn effect is a nice bonus, and the DEF increase helps her tank. Another great F2P option is **We Are Wildfire** for its team DMG Reduction.

## FAQ

**1. What is the best build for Fu Xuan in HSR?**
The best **Fu Xuan Build** focuses on maximizing HP and DEF, with an Energy Regeneration Rate Rope. Aim for pieces from the **Passerby of Wandering Cloud** and **Guard of Wuthering Snow** relic sets. Her signature Light Cone, **She Already Shut Her Eyes**, is BiS, but **Moment of Victory** is an excellent 4-star alternative.

**2. What relics should I use for Fu Xuan?**
The optimal **Fu Xuan Relics** are a 2-piece/2-piece mix of **Passerby of Wandering Cloud** (HP+10%) and **Guard of Wuthering Snow** (DMG Reduction). For Planar Ornaments, **Fleet of the Ageless** is the universal best choice, providing HP and a team ATK buff. For main stats, prioritize HP% on Body, SPD on Feet, HP% or DEF% on Sphere, and Energy Regeneration Rate on Rope.

**3. What is Fu Xuan's best light cone?**
Her signature Light Cone, **She Already Shut Her Eyes**, is unquestionably her **Fu Xuan Best Light Cone**, offering perfect stats and effects. For free-to-play players, **Moment of Victory** from the Herta Store is the top recommendation, followed by **Trend of the Universal Market**.

**4. What team is best for Fu Xuan?**
The **Fu Xuan Best Team** in the current meta is often a Mono-Quantum setup with **Silver Wolf** and **Sparkle**. However, she is incredibly flexible. She pairs amazingly with HP-scaling DPS like **Blade** and enables hypercarry teams by providing all necessary defensive buffs alone.

**5. Is Fu Xuan good in Honkai Star Rail?**
Yes, **Honkai Star Rail Fu Xuan** is considered one of the best characters in the game. She is a top-tier Preservation unit who simplifies endgame content like Memory of Chaos by providing exceptional teamwide mitigation, HP buffs, and offensive support, all in one unit.

**6. What does Fu Xuan scale with in HSR?**
**HSR Fu Xuan's** damage and the Max HP buff she provides to the team scale directly with **her own Max HP**. Her survivability, however, depends on both **Max HP and DEF**. She has no meaningful scaling with ATK.

**7. Is Fu Xuan a DPS or support?**
Fu Xuan is definitively a **support**—specifically, a defensive support or tank on the Preservation Path. While her Ultimate deals significant AoE damage (scaling with HP), her primary role is to protect and enhance her team's survivability and offensive stats.

**8. Is Fu Xuan worth pulling in 2026?**
Absolutely. The value of a top-tier sustain unit like **Fu Xuan** never diminishes. Her kit's utility in damage mitigation and team buffing is timeless, making her a valuable investment for any account struggling with survival in endgame content or looking to optimize clear speeds.

**9. How to build Fu Xuan for maximum damage?**
To build **Fu Xuan** for maximum damage, you must still prioritize her survival stats (HP, DEF), as her damage scales with HP. Equip her with HP% Main Stats on Body and Sphere. Consider using **4-Piece Longevous Disciple** relics if paired with a HP-draining DPS like Blade to gain extra CRIT Rate. Use an Energy Regeneration Rope to ensure frequent Ultimate usage for her HP-scaling AoE attack.

**10. What is Fu Xuan's best F2P light cone?**
Her best F2P Light Cone is **Moment of Victory** from the Herta Bond Light Cone shop. It provides high DEF and increases her aggro, which synergizes well with her role as a tank. **We Are Wildfire** is another viable option for its team-wide DMG Reduction effect.

By understanding these core aspects of **Fu Xuan**, you can fully leverage one of Honkai: Star Rail's most formidable defenders to conquer any challenge the game throws at you.
