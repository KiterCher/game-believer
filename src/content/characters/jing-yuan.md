# H1: Jing Yuan Guide — Best Builds, Teams & More | GameBeliever

## H2: Jing Yuan, the Shuhu of the Cloud Knights: An Overview

Jing Yuan, the 5-star Lightning character following the Path of Erudition, is one of Honkai Star Rail's most iconic and powerful AoE damage dealers. Released in version 1.0, he remains a cornerstone of the meta for players seeking overwhelming area-of-effect destruction. His rarity and unique playstyle make him a premium "Jing Yuan Build" investment.

**Role in the Meta:** Jing Yuan is a hyper-carry DPS who thrives in modes requiring sustained and burst AoE damage. His power is channeled almost entirely through his powerful summon, the Lightning Lord, making him a distinct "Jing Yuan Erudition" unit unlike any other.

**Key Strengths:**
*   **The Lightning Lord Mechanic:** His defining feature. This powerful summon acts on its own, delivering multiple hits of massive Lightning damage, providing exceptional off-turn damage and scaling.
*   **Exceptional AoE Coverage:** Both his Skill and Ultimate hit all enemies, making him ideal for clearing waves and damaging multiple elite enemies simultaneously.
*   **Strong Scaling with Stacks:** His damage ramps up significantly with the number of "Lightning-Lord's Favour" stacks he accumulates, rewarding strategic play.

**Weaknesses:**
*   **Stack Management Required:** He is heavily reliant on his Skill to build stacks for the Lightning Lord. Mismanagement can lead to significantly lower damage output.
*   **Vulnerable to Control Effects:** As his damage comes from his summon, if Jing Yuan himself is Imprisoned, Frozen, or otherwise crowd-controlled, the Lightning Lord will not act, severely disrupting his damage rotation.
*   **Energy-Dependent Burst:** His Ultimate, which also grants a stack to the Lightning Lord, requires careful energy management to align with his burst windows.

**What Makes Him Special?** Unlike other Erudition characters who cast spells directly, Jing Yuan's power is delegated to his summon. This creates a unique "delayed burst" gameplay where you build stacks over time to unleash a devastating sequence of attacks. This makes him feel more like a tactical commander than a standard spellcaster.

## H2: Jing Yuan's Skills Breakdown: Mastering the Lightning Lord

Understanding how to feed and empower the Lightning Lord is the core of any effective **Jing Yuan Guide**.

*   **Basic Attack (Rippling Spark):** Deals Lightning DMG to a single enemy. Its primary use is for generating Skill Points when his Skill is not needed or to carefully manage stacks.
*   **Skill (Spirit Invocation: Starfall):** Deals Lightning DMG to all enemies and grants him one stack of **"Lightning-Lord's Favour"** (max 10 stacks). This is his core ability for building the Lightning Lord's power. Use it every turn unless SP management is critical.
*   **Ultimate (Lightfall - Star of the Emperor):** Deals massive Lightning DMG to all enemies and grants him 2 stacks of **"Lightning-Lord's Favour."** This is his primary burst tool and stack accelerator. Timing it before the Lightning Lord's action maximizes damage.
*   **Talent (The General's Divine Foresight):** This is the heart of his kit. The **Lightning Lord** is a summon that acts after Jing Yuan's turn. Its number of hits (from 2 to 10) and its DMG multiplier are directly increased by the stacks of **"Lightning-Lord's Favour."** It attacks a random enemy for each hit, prioritizing those with lower HP.
*   **Technique (Chariot of Lightning):** After using Technique, creates a dimension that deals Lightning DMG to enemies, with a chance to shock them. This provides a strong opening in battle, helping to chip enemies and apply a Damage over Time (DoT) effect.

**Unique Mechanic Highlight:** The "delayed burst" of the Lightning Lord. Your main goal is to have as many stacks as possible *before* the Lightning Lord acts. A fully stacked, 10-hit Lightning Lord is one of the most devastating attacks in the game.

## H2: How Jing Yuan Fits in the Meta: The Undisputed AoE King

Despite the arrival of new DPS units, **Honkai Star Rail Jing Yuan** has maintained his status as a top-tier AoE DPS. His **"Jing Yuan Lightning"** damage profile is highly valuable.

**Current Meta Position:** He is a premier choice for Memory of Chaos, Pure Fiction, and events where you face multiple elite enemies or dense waves. He excels in scenarios that demand both AoE clearing and high single-target burst (via the Lightning Lord focusing hits).

**Best Teams:** He performs best in teams that can accelerate his stack generation, protect him from CC, or provide buffs that enhance his massive damage windows. A **"Jing Yuan Best Team"** often includes a harmony support like Bronya or Sparkle (to advance his turn and grant extra actions), a defensive support like Fu Xuan or Huohuo, and another harmonizer like Tingyun (for ATK buff and Energy).

**Content He Dominates:**
*   **Pure Fiction:** The premier AoE mode where his multi-target skills and Lightning Lord shine.
*   **Memory of Chaos (AoE Stages):** Perfect for stages with multiple elite enemies.
*   **Weekly Bosses with Adds:** Efficiently clears minions while focusing the main threat.

**Comparison to Similar Characters:** Compared to other Erudition characters like Herta or Qingque, Jing Yuan offers more consistent and higher burst potential thanks to the Lightning Lord. While characters like Acheron might have higher burst ceilings in specific teams, Jing Yuan's off-turn damage from his summon provides unparalleled pressure and flexibility. ## H2: Best Team Compositions for Jing Yuan: Assembling the Cavalry

A strong **"Jing Yuan Team"** is built around protecting him and enabling his stack-building.

1.  **The Classic Hypercarry (Jing Yuan / Bronya / Tingyun / Sustain):** This is the quintessential **"Jing Yuan Best Team."** Tingyun provides ATK buffs and much-needed Energy to fuel his Ultimate. Bronya uses her Skill to advance Jing Yuan's turn, letting him use his Skill (or even Ultimate) twice in a row for rapid stack generation. A sustain like Luocha or Fu Xuan keeps everyone alive. 3.  **F2P-Friendly & Effective (Jing Yuan / Asta / Tingyun / Sustain):** Asta's speed buff is incredible for letting Jing Yuan lap turns and build stacks. Paired with Tingyun's buffs, this team can propel Jing Yuan to new heights even without premium 5-star supports.

## H2: FAQ: Your Jing Yuan Questions Answered

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions about building and using the Divine Foresight.

1.  **What is the best build for Jing Yuan in HSR?**
    The best **"Jing Yuan Build"** prioritizes Crit Rate/Damage and ATK%. He typically uses the **Inert Salsotto** relic set for its follow-up attack boost, paired with a set like **Musketeer of Wild Wheat**. His Light Cone should be a high-stat Erudition cone, with his signature **Before Dawn** being the ideal.

2.  **What relics should I use for Jing Yuan?**
    The optimal **"Jing Yuan Relics"** are a 2-piece **Inert Salsotto** (for Crit Rate and Ultimate/Summon DMG) and a 2-piece set like **Musketeer of Wild Wheat** (for ATK and SPD) or **The Wastelander of Banditry Desert** (for Crit DMG vs debuffed enemies). Aim for Crit DMG on the body, ATK% on the boots, Lightning DMG on the sphere, and ATK% on the rope.

3.  **What is Jing Yuan's best light cone?**
    His Signature Light Cone, **Before Dawn**, is his undisputed best-in-slot. It provides a large Crit DMG stat and a powerful effect that boosts his Ultimate and Talent (Lightning Lord) damage. Among gacha options, **Night on the Milky Way** and **The Seriousness of Breakfast** are excellent alternatives. See our **[Light Cone Recommendations](/light-cone/recommendations)** for more.

4.  **What team is best for Jing Yuan?**
    The **"Jing Yuan Best Team"** typically includes a turn-advancer or buffer like **Bronya** or **Sparkle**, an energy provider/ATK buffer like **Tingyun**, and a reliable sustain character. This setup maximizes his stack generation and damage potential.

5.  **Is Jing Yuan good in Honkai Star Rail?**
    Yes, absolutely. **HSR Jing Yuan** is a top-tier AoE DPS who excels in endgame content like Memory of Chaos and Pure Fiction. His unique off-turn damage from the Lightning Lord makes him exceptionally strong.

6.  **What does Jing Yuan scale with in HSR?**
    **Jing Yuan** scales primarily with **ATK** and **Crit Stats (Rate/DMG)**. His damage is further enhanced by buffs that increase Lightning DMG, follow-up attack DMG, and generic DMG bonuses. The number of stacks for his Lightning Lord is also a critical scaling factor.

7.  **Is Jing Yuan a DPS or support?**
    Jing Yuan is unequivocally a **hyper-carry DPS**. His entire kit is designed to deal massive damage, primarily through his powerful summon, the Lightning Lord.

8.  **What is Jing Yuan's best F2P light cone?**
    For free-to-play players, **The Seriousness of Breakfast** (available from Herta's shop) is his best option. It provides a solid ATK% buff that stacks with enemy defeats. **Today Is Another Peaceful Day** from the Battle Pass is also a very strong alternative if available.
