## Aventurine: The Fortuitous Shield

**Aventurine** is a 5★ Imaginary character on the **Path of Preservation** who debuted in Version 2.1. Unlike traditional tanks that focus purely on survivability, this IPC Executive introduces a dynamic, offensive twist to the **Preservation** role. He functions as a hybrid shielder and sub-DPS, bringing both robust team protection and consistent follow-up damage.

**Key Strengths:**
*   **Offensive Shielding:** His unique scaling with DEF allows him to generate powerful shields while simultaneously dealing significant personal damage, a rare trait among tanks.
*   **Follow-Up Attack Engine:** Aventurine’s kit enables a constant stream of follow-up attacks, making him excellent for breaking toughness bars and exploiting follow-up attack bonuses.
*   **Imprisonment Utility:** His Ultimate inflicts Imprisonment, a powerful crowd-control effect that delays enemy actions and breaks Imaginary weakness, adding a layer of tactical control.

**Weaknesses:**
*   **Reliant on Enemy Attacks:** His talent, which grants him the Fortunatus stack needed for follow-up attacks, requires him to be hit. In fights with few enemies or slow attack patterns, his performance can dip.
*   **No Direct Healing:** Unlike other Preservation units, Aventurine cannot heal the team. His protection is solely shield-based, requiring careful management or pairing with a healer in highly damaging content.
*   **Skill Point Positive?** Not entirely. While his basic attacks are skill-point positive, maintaining maximum shields and triggering his follow-ups demands his Ultimate and timely skill use.

**What Sets Him Apart:** While characters like **Fu Xuan** mitigate damage and **Gepard** provides massive pre-emptive shields, Aventurine is the premier **offensive Preservation** unit. His ability to contribute meaningfully to team damage while shielding makes him uniquely valuable in fast-paced, multi-phase battles where every point of damage counts. For players seeking a tank that does more than just absorb hits, **Honkai Star Rail Aventurine** is a game-changer.

## Aventurine's Skills: A Breakdown of Fortuity

Understanding Aventurine’s kit is key to maximizing his potential as an **Aventurine Preservation** powerhouse.

*   **Basic Attack: Straight Bet**
    Deals Imaginary DMG to a single target. Its primary use is for Skill Point generation and minor chip damage. At high Trace levels, it gains a chance to apply a small shield to an ally, adding a layer of utility.

*   **Skill: Cornerstone Deluxe**
    Aventurine's core defensive tool. He grants a substantial shield to the entire team based on his DEF. The shield's strength is the foundation of his role, protecting allies from devastating AoE attacks. Using this Skill at the right time is crucial for survival.

*   **Ultimate: Roulette Shark**
    Aventurine’s offensive and control centerpiece. He deals massive **Imaginary DMG** to all enemies, with a scaling chance to **Imprison** them based on his Effect Hit Rate. This ability not only provides a huge burst of damage but also controls the battlefield by delaying enemy turns and breaking Imaginary Weakness.

*   **Talent: Jackpot Deal**
    The engine behind his unique playstyle. When an ally (including himself) is attacked, Aventurine gains a stack of Fortunatus, up to a maximum. At maximum stacks, his next attack triggers a powerful follow-up attack that hits all enemies. This makes him excellent against aggressive enemy lineups and in modes like Pure Fiction.

*   **Technique: Strategic Investment**
    Creates a dimension that increases the team's All Type RES for a duration after entering battle. This pre-emptive buff enhances the team's survivability right from the start, making it perfect for challenging Memory of Chaos floors.

## The Aventurine Build: Balancing Defense and Offense

Building Aventurine revolves around maximizing his Defense (DEF) to scale both his shields and his damage. This is what makes the **Aventurine Build** so compelling—you're investing in survivability and damage simultaneously.

**Primary Stat Goals:**
*   **Body:** DEF% or Crit Rate/Crit DMG (if using his Signature Light Cone).
*   **Feet:** SPD for more turns and energy, or DEF% for stronger shields.
*   **Planar Sphere:** DEF% is king.
*   **Link Rope:** DEF% for raw power or Energy Regeneration Rate for more frequent Ultimates and shield uptime.

**Why This Build Works:** Every point of DEF you stack on Aventurine serves a dual purpose. It directly increases the strength of the shield from his Skill *and* boosts the damage from his Ultimate and follow-up attacks via his A6 Major Trace. ## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. What is the best build for Aventurine in HSR?**
The **Aventurine Best Build** focuses on stacking DEF% to maximize both his shield strength and personal damage. Aim for a 2-piece **Knight of Purity Palace** set with a 2-piece **Wastelander of Banditry Desert** or **The Belobog Architect** set. His signature Light Cone is ideal, but strong F2P options exist.

**2. What relics should I use for Aventurine?**
For Aventurine Relics, the 4-piece **Knight of Purity Palace** is often recommended for its massive DEF% boost, directly scaling his shields and damage. For Planar Ornaments, **Inert Salsotto** boosts his follow-up and Ultimate damage, while **Belobog of the Architects** provides pure DEF and Effect Hit Rate.

**3. What is Aventurine's best light cone?**
His Signature Light Cone, **Inherently Unjust Destiny**, is his best in slot, providing DEF, Follow-Up attack DMG, and a team-wide DEF buff. Excellent alternatives include **Moment of Victory** (from the Standard Banner) and the 4-star **Day One of My New Life**.

**4. What team is best for Aventurine?**
The **Aventurine Best Team** is typically the Follow-Up Attack comp with Dr. Ratio and Topaz & Numby. However, he is flexible and fits well into any team needing a tank that contributes to damage, such as with Imbibitor Lunae or as a universal support for any hypercarry.

**5. Is Aventurine good in Honkai Star Rail?**
Yes, the **Honkai Star Rail Aventurine** is considered an excellent, S-tier Preservation character. His unique blend of strong shielding, personal damage, crowd control, and follow-up attacks makes him highly valuable for endgame content.

**6. What does Aventurine scale with in HSR?**
Aventurine primarily scales with **DEF**. Both his shield strength (Skill) and his damage (Ultimate, follow-up attack via Major Trace A6) scale directly off his Defense stat. Some of his damage also scales with Crit stats.

**7. Is Aventurine a DPS or support?**
He is a hybrid **Preservation** unit that blends tank (support) and sub-DPS roles. While his primary job is to shield the team (support), a significant portion of his kit and value comes from dealing damage, making him an offensive support.

**8. What is Aventurine's best F2P light cone?**
For F2P players, the 4-star **Day One of My New Life** (from the Herta's Store) is fantastic, providing DEF and a team-wide damage reduction buff. The 3-star **Pioneering** can also work in a pinch for extra DEF.

**9. How to build Aventurine for maximum damage?**
To build Aventurine for maximum damage, focus on **DEF%**, **Crit Rate**, and **Crit DMG** on your relics and light cone. Use the **Inert Salsotto** planar set. Ensure his major traces are unlocked, as his A6 trace is what converts his DEF into significant bonus damage on follow-up attacks.

**10. Is Aventurine worth pulling in 2026?**
Absolutely. As of the current meta, **HSR Aventurine** is a top-tier, future-proof unit. His unique kit addresses a niche no other character perfectly fills, making him a fantastic investment for your account, especially for tackling the most challenging endgame content.
