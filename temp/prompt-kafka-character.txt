## Description
Kafka is a 5-star Lightning character following the Path of Nihility. She is a core member of the Stellaron Hunters and excels as a DoT (Damage over Time) specialist who can apply and detonate Shock effects on enemies.

## Skills
### Basic Attack: Midnight Threnody
Deals Lightning DMG equal to 50%~130% of Kafka's ATK to a single enemy.

### Skill: Caressing Moonlight
Deals Lightning DMG equal to 80%~200% of Kafka's ATK to a single enemy and Lightning DMG equal to 30%~75% of Kafka's ATK to adjacent enemies. There is a 100% base chance to inflict Shock on the target and adjacent enemies for 2 turns.

### Ultimate: Twilight Trill
Deals Lightning DMG equal to 48%~120% of Kafka's ATK to all enemies. Immediately causes DoT effects on the target to deal 1 instance of DMG equal to 75%~100% of their original DMG.
Energy Cost: 120

### Talent: Gentle but Cruel
After an ally uses Basic ATK on an enemy target, Kafka immediately launches a follow-up attack, dealing Lightning DMG equal to 42%~105% of Kafka's ATK to that enemy. This effect can only be triggered 1 time per turn.

### Technique: Mercy Is No Mercy
Immediately attacks the enemy. After entering battle, deals Lightning DMG equal to 50% of Kafka's ATK to all enemies, with a 100% base chance to inflict Shock on all enemies for 3 turns.
