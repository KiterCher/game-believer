## Description
Kafka is a 5-star Lightning character following the Path of Nihility. She is a core member of the Stellaron Hunters and excels as a DoT (Damage over Time) specialist who can apply and detonate Shock effects on enemies.

## Skills
### Basic Attack: Midnight Threnody
Deals Lightning DMG equal to 50%~130% of Kafka's ATK to a single enemy.

### Skill: Caressing Moonlight
Deals Lightning DMG equal to 80%~200% of Kafka's ATK to a single enemy and Lightning DMG equal to 30%~75% of Kafka's ATK to adjacent enemies. There is a 100% base chance to inflict Shock on the target and adjacent enemies for 2 turns.

### Ultimate: Twilight Trill
Deals Lightning DMG equal to 48%~120% of Kafka's ATK to all enemies. Immediately causes DoT effects on the target to deal 1 instance of DMG equal to 75%~100% of their original DMG.
Energy Cost: 120

### Talent: Gentle but Cruel
After an ally uses Basic ATK on an enemy target, Kafka immediately launches a follow-up attack, dealing Lightning DMG equal to 42%~105% of Kafka's ATK to that enemy. This effect can only be triggered 1 time per turn.

### Technique: Mercy Is No Mercy
Immediately attacks the enemy. After entering battle, deals Lightning DMG equal to 50% of Kafka's ATK to all enemies, with a 100% base chance to inflict Shock on all enemies for 3 turns.

## Major Traces
- **Plunder** (A2): If an enemy target is currently receiving DoT, when Kafka's Skill hits this enemy, she deals additional Lightning DMG equal to 25% of her ATK to this enemy.
- **Fortissimo** (A4): When using her Ultimate, Kafka's ATK increases by 30% for 2 turns.
- **Tick-Tock** (A6): When entering battle, if there are teammates following the Path of Nihility, Kafka's DMG increases by 30% for each Nihility teammate (up to 2).

## Eidolons
- **E1 Recitatif of Crepuscule**: When an enemy target with Shock is attacked by Kafka, the target's Shock status immediately deals 1 instance of DMG equal to 30% of the original DMG.
- **E2 Recitatif of Fishes**: When an ally is attacked, Kafka has a 100% base chance to inflict Shock on the attacker for 2 turns. This effect can only trigger 1 time per turn.
- **E3 Recitatif of Cocoons**: Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.
- **E4 Recitatif of Something**: When Kafka's Talent triggers a follow-up attack, there is a 100% base chance to inflict Shock on the enemy for 2 turns.
- **E5 Recitatif of Birds**: Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Talent Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.
- **E6 Recitatif of the Beginning**: When using Ultimate, the DMG multiplier for immediately triggering DoT increases by 50%.
