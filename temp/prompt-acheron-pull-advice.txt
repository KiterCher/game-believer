## Description
Acheron is a 5-star Lightning character following the Path of Nihility. She is a mysterious Galaxy Ranger who wields a sword and excels as a hypercarry DPS with unique Slash mechanics that bypass enemy defenses.

## Skills
### Basic Attack: Trilateral Slash
Deals Lightning DMG equal to 50%~130% of Acheron's ATK to a single enemy.

### Skill: Octobolt Flash
Deals Lightning DMG equal to 84%~210% of Acheron's ATK to a single enemy and gains 1 point of Slashed Dream. If the enemy is inflicted with debuff, gains 1 additional point of Slashed Dream.

### Ultimate: Slashed Dream Cries in Red
When Acheron has 3 points of Slashed Dream, she can use Ultimate. Deals Lightning DMG multiple times to all enemies. Each hit has a 100% base chance to inflict Crimson Knot on enemies. When Crimson Knot is removed, deals additional Lightning DMG equal to a percentage of Acheron's ATK.
Energy Cost: 0

### Talent: The Abyss
When an ally uses an ability that inflicts debuff on an enemy, Acheron gains 1 point of Slashed Dream. At the start of each wave, gains 1 point of Slashed Dream. When Acheron has 3 points of Slashed Dream, her Ultimate becomes available.

### Technique: Quadrilateral Ascension
After using Technique, immediately attacks enemies in a large area. After entering battle, deals Lightning DMG equal to 100% of Acheron's ATK to all enemies and gains 1 point of Slashed Dream.

## Major Traces
- **Red Oni** (A2): When Acheron's Slashed Dream reaches maximum, her CRIT Rate increases by 15% for 2 turns.
- **Blue Oni** (A4): When an ally inflicts a debuff on an enemy, Acheron's DMG increases by 30% for 2 turns.
- **The Storm** (A6): At the start of battle, Acheron gains 1 point of Slashed Dream. When Acheron uses Ultimate, her CRIT DMG increases by 50% for 1 turn.

## Eidolons
- **E1 Lone Star**: When battle starts, Acheron gains 1 point of Slashed Dream. When Acheron's Slashed Dream is at maximum, her CRIT Rate increases by 15%.
- **E2 Mara**: When an ally uses an ability that inflicts debuff on an enemy, Acheron gains 1 additional point of Slashed Dream. This effect can only trigger 1 time per turn.
- **E3 Red Banner**: Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.
- **E4 Crimson Plum**: When Acheron uses Ultimate, her ATK increases by 50% for 2 turns.
- **E5 Violet Spark**: Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Talent Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.
- **E6 Final Dream**: When Acheron uses Ultimate, she ignores 20% of enemy DEF. Additionally, each hit of Ultimate has a 50% fixed chance to remove 1 Crimson Knot from the enemy.
