## Description
Firefly is a 5-star Fire character following the Path of Destruction. She is a member of the Stellaron Hunters who pilots the mecha SAM and excels as a Break Effect DPS with enhanced state mechanics.

## Skills
### Basic Attack: Order: Flare Propulsion
Deals Fire DMG equal to 50%~130% of Firefly's ATK to a single enemy.

### Skill: Order: Aerial Bombardment
Consumes 40% of Firefly's current HP. Deals Fire DMG equal to 100%~250% of Firefly's ATK to a single enemy and adjacent enemies. If current HP is insufficient, HP drops to 1 instead.

### Ultimate: Fyrefly Type-IV: Complete Combustion
Enters Complete Combustion state for 3 turns. In this state, Firefly's SPD increases by 50, her Skill becomes Enhanced Skill, and her Basic ATK becomes Enhanced Basic ATK. When entering Complete Combustion, immediately takes action.
Energy Cost: 240

### Talent: Chrysalid Pyron
When Firefly's current HP percentage is 50% or lower, her Break Effect increases by 50%~125%. After using Enhanced Skill, if the enemy is Weakness Broken, deals additional Fire DMG equal to a percentage of Firefly's Break Effect.

### Technique: Order: Meteoric Incineration
After using Technique, Firefly enters battle with 50% of her Max HP consumed and immediately enters Complete Combustion state for 3 turns.

- **Molten Core** (A4): When Firefly's current HP is 50% or lower, her DMG increases by 40%.
- **Fuel Injection** (A6): After entering Complete Combustion state, Firefly's Weakness Break Efficiency increases by 50% for 3 turns.
